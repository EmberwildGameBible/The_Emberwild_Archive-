# 43.2 — WHAT IS AN ORBIT LINE?

An Orbit Line is the first dynamic, continuously moving Spirit Pattern.

Unlike Tri-Lines (static) or Crossforms (geometric anchors),

Orbit Lines represent motion-bound geometric intelligence.

### Definition:

A curved or elliptical path traced repeatedly by one or more Vessels,

following invisible geometric rails projected through the environment.

### Properties:

- smooth, unnatural perfect curves
- constant velocity
- identical repeat cycles (exact timing, exact angles)
- occasional flickers of light along the path
- slight magnetic pull toward the center point

Orbit Lines are boundaries.

Dividers.

Traces of something being drawn in the world.

They are the first sign of Spirit orbitals — patterns that will later form Nexus Rings.
