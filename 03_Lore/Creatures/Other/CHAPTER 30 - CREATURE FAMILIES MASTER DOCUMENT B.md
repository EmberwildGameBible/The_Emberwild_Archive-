# FAMILY 1 — WOLVES

“The forest breathes, and wolves are its pulse.”

Wolves are pack tacticians — the baseline predators of Emberwild.

### Biology & Ecology

Habitats: Forests, plains, tundra foothills

Diet: deer, rabbits, carrion

Behavior: Pack hunting, territorial, organized, stamina-driven

Seasonal: Winter aggression, Autumn migration

### Tiers

- Normal: moderate wolves (packs of 2–4)
- Rare: unique coats, better stamina
- Elite: large, scarred pack leaders
- Rare Elite: dominant alphas with deadly precision
- Mini-Boss: rogue alphas, handcrafted AI
- Boss: dungeon wolves (cave dens)

### Tracking

Pawprints, scat, kill sites, howls, den scratches

### Behavior / AI

Flanking, circling, coordinated rushes, morale tied to leader

### Weather

Fog = deadly, Snow = slow but easier tracks

### Combat Profile

Bites, rakes, lunge, pack synergy

Weakness: fire, morale breaks

### Loot

Pelts, fangs, sinew, Alpha parts

### Companions

Yes: Normal → Elite

Roles: Flanker, DPS, Tracking Assist

### World Role

Defines baseline predator difficulty.

# FAMILY 2 — BEARS

“When a bear rises, the whole wilderness holds its breath.”

Heavy predators with enormous strength.

### Biology

Habitats: forests, rivers, mountains

Diet: berries, fish, meat, carrion

Behavior: solitary, territorial, dangerous when startled

### Tiers

- Normal: strong but predictable
- Rare: large, unique coat
- Elite: massive old bears
- Rare Elite: huge, ancient specimens
- Mini-Boss: “Oldstone” class
- Boss: cave/ruin bears

### Tracking

Deep prints, claw marks, dens, overturned logs

### Behavior

Charge → slam, bite, swipe

Flee logic minimal

Weather: Rain = roam; Winter = conserve

### Combat

Highest raw melee damage

### Loot

Heavy hides, claws, hearts

### Companion

Yes: Normal → Elite

Role: Tank / Guard

### World Role

The “heavy danger” of the wilderness.
