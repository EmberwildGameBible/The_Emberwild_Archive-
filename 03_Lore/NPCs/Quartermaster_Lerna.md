# Quartermaster_Lerna

**Origin/Provenance:** Windbreak veteran who ran Saltmarrow docks before taking Timberline Post ledgers.

**Cultural Tie:** Speaks in clipped ledger code; respects Marrowhook couriers who balance loads by habit.

**Gameplay Hook:** Sells [Windbreak_Ice_Lantern](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Windbreak_Ice_Lantern.md) and [Briar_Sinew_Binding](../../08_Items/Crafting/Briar_Sinew_Binding.md); offers discounts after Slidecaller runs.

**Voice Notes:** “Pay in resin or salt, I don’t mind which. But don’t bring me ash charms without a story.”
