# SECTION H.0 — PURPOSE OF THE SPIRIT REALM

The Spirit Realm is not a magic world, not a combat dimension, and not a power-fantasy plane.

It is:

- an echo of Emberwild
- a memory layer of the land
- a way to see what once was
- a tool for tracking, lore, exploration, and secrets
- a lightweight environmental system, not a boss arena

It is accessed through a single, controlled mechanic:

### The Eye of the Spirit Realm

(an item that temporarily reveals the “memory layer” of the world)

The Spirit Realm is passive, environmental, atmospheric, and exists to deepen:

- exploration
- tracking
- world-history
- puzzle-solving
- rare discovery
- spiritual insight, not magic power

This is a realistic animist layer, not a supernatural combat system.

────────────────────────

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.1 — WHAT THE SPIRIT REALM ACTUALLY IS

The Spirit Realm is:

### A memory overlay of the world

Not a separate dimension.

Not a zone transition.

Not a teleportation.

When activated:

- the world shifts colors
- sound muffles
- ambient echoes appear
- old footprints show
- ancient paths light up
- faint shapes of creatures linger
- certain hidden materials glow subtly

It is a visual filter revealing:

- old events
- lingering energies
- residue of living creatures
- soul-prints
- spirit flora

This is not magic — it is ancestral memory.

────────────────────────

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.2 — WHAT IT IS NOT

To protect the tone of Emberwild, the Spirit Realm must NEVER become:

- a combat mode
- a power-up state
- a loot farming exploit
- a magical world full of bosses
- a supernatural spell plane
- a replacement for realistic gameplay
- a damage type or combat school

If it veers magical, the entire identity of your game collapses.

This rewrite ensures that never happens.

────────────────────────

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.3 — HOW THE PLAYER ENTERS THE SPIRIT REALM

Players cannot enter freely.

Instead, they use:

### The Eye of the Spirit Realm

A crafted, rare, region-bound item that:

- creates a temporary Spirit Sight
- reveals echoes for 10–20 seconds
- has a long cooldown
- requires ritual materials to craft
- is maintained by local shamans, priests, or monks (depending on heritage)

The Eye does NOT allow:

- permanent spirit sight
- combat advantage
- combat detection
- ghost-fighting

It is purely a tool.

────────────────────────

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.4 — WHAT PLAYERS SEE IN THE SPIRIT REALM

When the Eye is active:

### 1. Echo Footprints

Footprints of:

- creatures (recent or ancient)
- rare beasts
- herds
- predators
- NPCs tied to quests
- poachers
- caravans
- companions (memory trails)

This enhances tracking dramatically.

---

### 2. Lingered Creature Silhouettes

Soft silhouettes of:

- Normal creatures (hazy)
- Rare creatures (distinct)
- Elite/Rare Elite (strong outlines)
- Mini-Boss ghosts (only as history, NEVER as fightable entities)

These are echoes, not enemies.

---

### 3. Forgotten Paths

You may see:

- hidden ruins
- old foundations
- erased trails
- lost hunting routes
- broken bridges
- collapsed mines
- ancient camps

These appear as:

- faint outlines
- shimmering pathways
- memory-lines carved into stone

---

### 4. Spirit Flora

Unique herbs/plants visible ONLY during Spirit Sight.

Examples:

- Moonlace
- Hollowroot
- Spiritbloom

These vanish once the Eye ends, but can be collected if you reach them in time.

---

### 5. Narrative Echoes

Silent echoes:

- long-dead NPCs
- spirit animals
- past events
- lost battles
- migrations
- rituals

They cannot be interacted with.

They exist purely to enrich the world.

# SECTION H.5 — DESIGN LIMITATIONS (VERY IMPORTANT)

To protect the grounded authenticity of Emberwild:

### No combat exists in the Spirit Realm.

### No creatures attack in the Spirit Realm.

### Players cannot damage or interact with echoes.

### You cannot “fight ghosts.”

### You cannot loot spirits.

This is a vision layer, not gameplay power.

────────────────────────

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.6 — SPIRIT REALM IN TRACKING

The Spirit Realm creates the highest form of tracking clarity:

### Rarity tracking cues:

- Normal → faint
- Rare → visible lines
- Elite → bright, bold footprints
- Rare Elite → full-body silhouette pulses
- Mini-Boss → ground-scar echoes (history only)

Spirit Sight does NOT reveal:

- exact creature location
- future movement
- creature HP
- loot tables

It simply confirms direction & history.

# SECTION H.7 — SPIRIT REALM MATERIALS & GATHERING

Certain rare resources are only visible during Spirit Sight.

These MUST remain grounded:

- spiritbloom (rare alchemy root)
- faded bark (used for incense)
- duskfeather down (from lingering avian echoes)
- hollowroot fungus

These materials fuel:

- story quests
- alchemy
- rituals
- companion bonding incense
- Eye maintenance

NOT combat power.

# SECTION H.8 — SPIRIT REALM & COMPANIONS

Companions behave differently:

### 1. They grow uneasy.

Animals react instinctively to the spirit layer.

### 2. Loyalty grows slightly if you explore the Spirit Realm safely together.

It strengthens their bond with you.

### 3. They may visibly track echoes you can’t see.

This is a powerful, grounded hint system.

### 4. Injured companions leave deeper spirit footprints.

Tamers gain more from this system than any other class.

────────────────────────

# SECTION H.9 — SPIRIT REALM DANGERS (NON-COMBAT)

The Spirit Realm remains non-combat.

Danger appears in subtle, atmospheric ways:

- distorted sound
- disorientation
- low-visibility
- old trauma sites
- misdirection on deep trails
- sudden silence
- cold-drop sensation

If overused:

- player screen may blur
- footsteps may echo incorrectly
- pathfinding can become misleading
- the Eye may become unstable (cooldown penalty)

This creates tension—without combat.
