# B116 – Greatwood Contract Pack (Phase III–IV, Levels 10–20)
Purpose

Define the mid/late Greatwood contract lineup (Levels ~10–20) that lives on top of:

Greatwood’s pockets & landmarks (B113)

Enemy families & behaviors (B114)

Onboarding phases (B111)

Roles & builds (B110)

Signature Hunts (B103)

Corruption & Warding (91, B101, B109)

Lodge Projects & Events (B102, B104)

These contracts:

Push the player into corruption pockets, Warren, Wardstone Hill, and deeper Verge/Ember Run.

Support and lead into Signature Hunts (B103).

Tie directly into Ember Feast & Gloaming Vigil.

Help define circle alignment and social identity (B105).

This pack covers Phase III–IV of B111:

Roughly Levels 10–20.

Contract Categories (Phase III–IV)

Corruption Hunts & Warding Actions

Advanced Research & Specimen Contracts

Circle-Specific Contracts (Huntmasters / Wardens / Scholars)

Signature Hunt Support Contracts

Vigil & Surge Contracts

Feast & Social-Event Adjacent Contracts

Wrap-Up / Transition Contracts (End of Greatwood)

Each contract: name, type, pocket, level band, blurb, objectives, system hooks.

1. Corruption Hunts & Warding Actions

These are the core “the forest is sick and you fix it” jobs.

C1 – “Seep at the Grove’s Edge”

Type: Corruption Hunt – Localized cleanse.

Pocket: Black Sap Grove (outer ring).

Level Band: 10–12.

Blurb

“The Grove’s edge is oozing onto good ground. Warden Aelric wants the worst of the seep burned back and the rest mapped.”

Objectives

Travel to 3× seep sites on Grove periphery.

At each site:

Kill Sap-Wolves and Sap Leech Swarms.

Burn Sapfall-like growths with fire or alchemical charges.

Place 2× Ward Markers (interactables) as instructed by Aelric.

Return to Warding Hall and update the ward map.

Systems

Introduces:

Light ward placement.

Uses:

Fire/Alchemy synergy (player encouraged to bring fire brews).

Moves:

Grove ZCL in that ring slightly down for a time.

C2 – “Stones in the Dark”

Type: Warding Action – Wardstone stabilization.

Pocket: Wardstone Hill (sub-areas around central stone).

Level Band: 11–14.

Blurb

“The minor ward-stones ringing the Hill are flickering. Aelric needs someone to stand watch while the apprentices reset them.”

Objectives

Escort a pair of Warden apprentices to 3× minor wardstones around the Hill.

At each:

Defend them against waves of Ridge Beasts and Ward-Twisted enemies while they channel.

Optional:

Use Warden gear/field wards to strengthen the circle (bonus Wardens rep).

Return and report.

Systems

Multi-stage escort/protect.

Heavy Warden flavor.

Ties:

Directly into ward map & future Gloaming Vigil strength.

C3 – “Rot Beneath the Roots”

Type: Corruption Hunt – Root incursion.

Pocket: Intersection of Ragged Verge & Grove fringe.

Level Band: 12–15.

Blurb

“Neris claims the sap isn’t just dripping; it’s reaching. Black roots have surfaced near the Verge. You’re to cut them out before they anchor.”

Objectives

Find 2–3 corrupted root clusters in specified Verge clearings.

Clear:

Resin Husks guarding them.

Sapling Horrors anchoring them.

Use Alchemical solvents provided by Neris to treat stumps.

Decide:

Hand soil samples to Neris or to Aelric (minor Lodge-Faithful vs Fringe choice).

Systems

Mixed Warden/Scholar conflict.

Corruption pocket slightly retracted or changed depending on who gets samples.

2. Advanced Research & Specimen Contracts

Scholars’ Circle contracts that lean heavily into Field Scribe and specimen gathering.

R4 – “Brood Ledger”

Type: Research – Hollowroot taxonomy.

Pocket: Hollowroot Fringe → shallow Warren.

Level Band: 10–13.

Blurb

“Elgath wants more than ‘big, chittering, awful’. Record types, numbers, and movements of Hollowroot brood before we go hacking at their Matron.”

Objectives

In Hollowroot Fringe & first Warren chambers:

Observe (tag) 3 brood types:

Grubs.

Stalkers.

Spore clusters.

Fill Field Scribe entries by:

Watching patterns.

Surviving 1–2 ambushes.

Optional:

Collect 1 pristine carapace (Trapper Adept check).

Return to Elgath for bestiary update.

Systems

Deepens:

Field Scribe.

Primes:

Hollowroot Matron understanding (B103).

R5 – “Songs of the Sap”

Type: Research – Black Sap resonance.

Pocket: Deeper Black Sap Grove (mid-ring).

Level Band: 13–16.

Blurb

“Neris insists the sap ‘sings different’ in deeper pools. Take her resonant lens, listen, and try not to let it in too deep.”

Objectives

Use a special lens item at 3× mid-Grove sap formations.

Each use:

Spawns a Sap Leech Swarm or Sapling Horror that must be dealt with.

Adds HCL increment (player must manage Corruption).

Optional:

Stay extra time at one site to obtain a Rare Resonant Sample (higher risk, better reward).

Return to Neris.

Systems

Strong:

HCL management.

Corruption as resource tension.

Unlocks:

Black Sap gear or corruption-scaling brews.

R6 – “Shifting Lines in the Ward”

Type: Research – Ward behavior mapping.

Pocket: Wardstone Hill & nearby forest.

Level Band: 14–17.

Blurb

“Elgath wants to chart where the ward is thinning. Aelric wants to know why. You get to walk the line and tell them both.”

Objectives

Carry a ward attunement device and walk a marked route around Wardstone Hill at night.

At 3–4 points, interact to:

Read ward strength.

Possibly trigger minor Ward Echo spawns.

Optional:

Share data with only one of:

Aelric (Warden boost, more defensive solutions).

Neris (Fringe boost, experimental solutions).

Turn report in via Elgath (who reacts to your choice).

Systems

Ward map interaction.

Night travel pressure.

Circle alignment influence.

3. Circle-Specific Contracts

These exist to reinforce your circle lean and give each circle “their” content in mid/late.

HM1 – “Trial of Three Marks” (Huntmasters)

Type: Circle Trial – Multi-mark.

Pocket: Ragged Verge, Ember Run, Hollowroot Fringe edge.

Level Band: 12–16.

Blurb

“Ruarc says you’re fit for a proper Huntmaster trial. Three marks, three pockets, no excuses.”

Objectives

Mark 1:

Hunt a large Ridge Beast in Ragged Verge (variant stag/boar).

Mark 2:

Hunt a river predator in Ember Run (croc-like beast).

Mark 3:

Hunt a brood beast skirting Hollowroot Fringe.

Optional:

Bring back distinct trophies from each for extra Huntmasters rep.

Systems

Tests:

Travel competence.

Versatility in different environments.

Grants:

Strong Huntmaster gear & title bump.

WD1 – “Stand on the Line” (Wardens)

Type: Circle Trial – Defensive stand.

Pocket: Mixed – watchposts at Crossroads, Grove edge, Warren Mouth.

Level Band: 13–17.

Blurb

“Aelric wants to know if you’re a hunter who stands when the line shakes. Take three posts. Don’t let them fall.”

Objectives

Defend Crossroads watchpost:

Waves of beasts from Ragged Verge.

Defend Grove edge watchpost:

Corrupted Sap-Wolves & Resin Husks.

Defend Warren Mouth watchpost:

Hollowroot Brood waves.

Keep Warden ward devices at each post above X HP.

Report to Aelric.

Systems

Multi-defense scenario.

Strong Warden rank reward.

SC1 – “Field Thesis: Greatwood at War” (Scholars)

Type: Circle Trial – Multi-part research.

Pocket: Multiple (Verge, Grove, Warren, Wardstone).

Level Band: 14–18.

Blurb

“Elgath proposes that Greatwood is ‘warring with itself’. He wants your notes to prove it.”

Objectives

Conduct short Field Scribe studies in:

Ragged Verge: beasts vs environment.

Black Sap Grove: corrupted vs uncorrupted flora/fauna.

Hollowroot Fringe: brood vs forest.

Wardstone Hill: ward echoes vs ambient life.

At each site:

Complete observation bar under danger.

Compile notes into a Field Thesis (UI moment).

Present to Elgath for evaluation.

Systems

Heavy Field Scribe progression.

Scholar circle rank & gear.

4. Signature Hunt Support Contracts

These prep the big fights, alter arenas, or reward knowledge.

SH-S1 – “Silence in the Ridge” (Howler Support)

Type: Pre-Signature Hunt – Ecosystem prep.

Pocket: Deep Ragged Verge.

Level Band: 10–13.

Blurb

“The Howler isn’t alone. Packs are circling its territory. Lyss wants them thinned before you go for the throat.”

Objectives

Culled X Ridge Wolves in Howler-adjacent clearings.

Destroy 2–3 den sites (interactable lairs).

Optional:

Plant Huntmaster markers for future use (slight Howler arena adjustment).

Report to Lyss.

Effects

In the actual Greatwood Howler fight:

Fewer wolves spawn in mid-phase.

Arena may have some pre-scouted safe cover depending on markers.

SH-S2 – “The Sapward Test” (Devourer Support)

Type: Pre-Signature Hunt – Ward experiment.

Pocket: Inner Black Sap Grove.

Level Band: 14–18.

Blurb

“Aelric believes we can plant a ward-totem that resists the sap. Neris insists it will explode. You’re going to go find out.”

Objectives

Escort a Warden sapward unit into deeper Grove.

Defend them vs:

Sap-Wolves.

Sapling Horrors.

Place 1–2 experimental ward totems.

Choose:

Follow Aelric’s stable configuration.

Or Neris’s riskier, more explosive version.

Return.

Effects

Devourer encounter:

Aelric path:

A small safe zone in arena with reduced Corruption.

Neris path:

Occasional volatile ward bursts that:

Damage both Devourer and player if misused.

SH-S3 – “Brood Sever” (Matron Support)

Type: Pre-Signature Hunt – Warren disruption.

Pocket: Hollowroot Warren mid-levels.

Level Band: 15–19.

Blurb

“Tamsin wants brood tunnels collapsed before you face the Matron. Less brood, more chances to breathe between her rages.”

Objectives

Enter mid-level Warren.

Plant alchemical charges at 3 brood tunnels indicated by Tamsin.

Defend each site while charges are set.

Trigger a controlled collapse (scripted sequence).

Escape to a safe chamber.

Effects

Matron fight:

Fewer brood waves.

Some old tunnels blocked, altering pathing and cover.

SH-S4 – “Whispers on the Hill” (Shadow Support)

Type: Pre-Signature Hunt – Investigation.

Pocket: Wardstone Hill & Lodge interior.

Level Band: 16–20.

Blurb

“Something’s wrong under the Wardstone, but Maera won’t move until she knows who is whispering into it. Time to listen.”

Objectives

Talk to:

Elgath.

Aelric.

Neris.

Investigate 3 suspicious Wardstone-adjacent scenes:

Strange offerings.

Ward glyph scratches.

Someone’s hidden notes.

Optional:

Decide whether to share everything with Maera, or hide some evidence (affects Shadow Under the Wardstone options).

Effects

Shadow encounter:

Branching:

Who is present.

How fully prepared you are.

Moral framing of options.

5. Vigil & Surge Contracts

Triggered around or during Gloaming Vigil (B104 + 91).

V1 – “Hold the Ember Bridge”

Type: Vigil Contract – Stand & defend.

Pocket: Ember Run – Ember Bridge.

Level Band: 14–18.

Blurb

“If the Ember Bridge falls, the Lodge loses half its quick routes. The Vigil says nothing crosses but water and the friends you bring.”

Objectives

Defend Ember Bridge from:

Mixed waves:

Ridge Beasts.

Corrupted variants.

Optional objectives:

Prevent bridge HP from dropping below X.

Use at least one Ward totem or Alchemy cloud to show “preparedness.”

Survive until Vigil timer ends for that night phase.

Systems

Vigil-specific UI and timer.

Direct effect on:

Vigil outcome quality.

Post-Vigil travel cost / ease.

V2 – “Lanterns in the Warren Mouth”

Type: Vigil Contract – Hazard sweep.

Pocket: Hollowroot Warren Mouth.

Level Band: 15–19.

Blurb

“If the Vigil is about watching, someone has to watch beneath. Set lanterns, burn spores, keep the brood from boiling up.”

Objectives

Place 3 Vigil Lanterns at Warren entrances.

Clear:

Brood that surge while you set them.

Burn visible spore patches.

Return to Warding Hall.

Systems

Light + darkness interplay.

Reduces:

Probability of brood surge events in Lodge during Vigil.

V3 – “Report of the Night”

Type: Vigil Contract – Summary & reflection.

Pocket: Lodge interior, Wardstone Hill.

Level Band: 16–20.

Blurb

“When the night passes, someone has to write down who stood and who didn’t. Elgath probably doesn’t sleep anyway.”

Objectives

Speak with:

Aelric.

Maera.

1–2 watchpost NPCs.

Contribute:

Your perspective on Vigil events (dialogue choices).

Generate:

A short Vigil Chronicle entry.

Turn it in to Elgath.

Systems

Social & narrative reflection.

Sets:

Flavor text in later expansions referencing this Vigil.

7. Wrap-Up / Transition Contracts (End of Greatwood)

Contracts that close the book on Greatwood and point outward.

X1 – “Settling the Ledger”

Type: Lodge Wrap-Up – Project & debt.

Pocket: Lodge interiors.

Level Band: 18–20.

Blurb

“Bran says the Lodge stands, which means the ledger has to balance. Time to see what you’ve really paid in and taken out.”

Objectives

Speak with Bran and review:

Lodge Projects completed.

Resources you contributed.

Optionally:

Fund one last small project (personal or common).

Receive:

A “Lodge Balance” summary.

Unlock:

A small permanent perk or decor piece that carries forward.

Systems

Gives player:

Retrospective sense of Lodge contribution.

Nice soft-canonization of how “their” Greatwood ended.

X2 – “The Last Walk Before the Road”

Type: Personal Wrap-Up – Farewell patrol.

Pocket: Player-chosen pockets (fast-travel via small chain).

Level Band: 18–20.

Blurb

“Lyss suggests one last look at the paths you’ve worn before you find new ones. The forest remembers your steps; you should too.”

Objectives

Visit 2–3 pockets where you spent the most time:

E.g., Ragged Verge, Grove, Warren, Ember Run.

At each:

Encounter a small reflection event:

An NPC comment.

A changed enemy presence.

A view of a ward or scar you helped create.

Close:

With a short, optional conversation at the Ridge Overlook with:

Lyss / Maera / a circle contact.

Contract completes as:

You approach the travel route out of Greatwood.

Systems

Purely narrative.

Ties:

Pockets & Hunts to emotional exit.
