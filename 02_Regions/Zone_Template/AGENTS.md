## Region depth rules
For any zone edit, include:
- 3 “daily life” details (food, labor, slang, superstition, petty crime)
- 2 micro-economy notes (imports/exports, tolls, smuggling, guild control)
- 1 travel hazard with a diegetic warning sign artifact
Always cross-link to at least 1 item, 1 quest, 1 faction.
