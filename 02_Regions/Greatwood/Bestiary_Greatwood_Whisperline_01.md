## Sap-Strider Matriarch
- Type: Beast
- Where Found: Resin Tangle
- Behaviors: Guards sap pools; slows foes with sticky sprays; charges when torches flare.
- Drops: Sap-Resin Salve, Resin Patch Kit, Tanglecut Hatchet, Sapstring Bandage.

## Milepost Whisperer
- Type: Humanoid (mysterious messenger)
- Where Found: Whisperline Milepost
- Behaviors: Leaves coded whispers; flees when lanterns brighten; trades for tokens.
- Drops: Smudged Route Permit, Roadling Trade Token, Murmurstone Charm, Ward-Chalk Packet.
- Threat Notes: Low damage but alerts smugglers; silencing reduces ambush chance.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Intercept whispers for wardens.
  - Deliver false routes for smugglers.

## Bracken Snare Captain
- Type: Humanoid (smuggler enforcer)
- Where Found: Bracken Snare Ring
- Behaviors: Sets snares mid-fight; calls in snareshots from trees; uses resin smoke.
- Drops: Smuggler's Knot Hook, Thornbar Sling, Smudged Route Permit, Coiled Line Spool.
- Threat Notes: Break snares with torches; watch for line pulls near brambles.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Burn snares to reopen the road.
  - Capture captain for warden interrogations.

- Quest Hooks:

  - Drive wisps off the road for safer escorts.
