# Greatwood Zone Index

Core references for Greatwood routes, lodge ties, and warded forests.

## Overview
Greatwood is a temperate woodland webbed with trade spurs and root-sung shrines. Wardens guard the ferry lines while smugglers test every hollow for hidden freight. Trails weave between sap collectors, shrine-keepers, and quarry-haulers who all listen for the forest's whisperline songs.
