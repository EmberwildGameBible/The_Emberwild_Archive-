## Chorus Bridge Singer
- Type: Humanoid (scout)
- Where Found: Chorus Bridge
- Behaviors: Taps planks to keep rhythm; warns allies with humming calls; lights torches when threatened.
- Drops: Chorus Bridge Torch, Sapstring Bandage, Rivergrain Porridge Mix, Coiled Line Spool.
- Threat Notes: Interrupt the song to reduce nearby patrol morale.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Escort singer to Sapstring Crossing.
  - Retrieve lost torch bundles from the creek.

## Whisperline Muleteer
- Type: Humanoid (traveler)
- Where Found: Sapstring Crossing, Whisperline Milepost
- Behaviors: Protects pack mules with hooked staffs; refuses to move at night unless paid in tokens.
- Drops: Roadling Trade Token, Whisperline Travel Ration, Crossing Guard's Satchel, Tanglecut Hatchet.
- Threat Notes: Will flee and call wardens; slowness debuff when mules panic.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Calm mules spooked by hook-runners.
  - Deliver bandages to keep the route open.

## Bridge Singer's Hound
- Type: Beast
- Where Found: Chorus Bridge approaches
- Behaviors: Barks when planks squeal; chases smugglers; drags rope to block crossings.
- Drops: Sap-Resin Salve, Bivouac Tent Stakes, Resin Patch Kit, Lowlight Shroom Tea.
- Threat Notes: Howl can fear low-level escorts; keep torches lit to deter.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Return the hound after it bolts toward Bracken Snare Ring.
  - Train it using rivergrain treats.

## Thornbar Ash Scribe
- Type: Humanoid (smuggler clerk)
- Where Found: Thornbar Bivouac
- Behaviors: Records bribes; tosses ash to blind foes; whistles for hook-runners.
- Drops: Smudged Route Permit, Ward-Chalk Packet, Smuggler's Knot Hook, Sapstring Bandage.
- Threat Notes: Blinding ash reduces accuracy; splash water to clear vision.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Steal the ash ledger before the bribe run.
  - Swap forged permits into their satchel.

## Cairn Hum Whisperer
- Type: Spirit
- Where Found: Murmur Wardstone
- Behaviors: Echoes back wrong lyrics; drains stamina when ignored; flits toward lit chalk lines.
- Drops: Murmurstone Charm, Cairnkeeper's Beads, Lowlight Shroom Tea, Ward-Chalk Packet.
- Threat Notes: Silence effects cut the hum; interrupt before it reaches full pitch.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Align grooves using a Rootsong Listening Horn.
  - Gather damp chalk after a ritual.

## Resin Tangle Sapling Borer
- Type: Vermin
- Where Found: Resin Tangle sap pools
- Behaviors: Chews at boot leather; tunnels into resin puddles; hides under fallen bark.
- Drops: Sap-Resin Salve, Bivouac Tent Stakes, Coiled Line Spool, Rivergrain Porridge Mix.
- Threat Notes: Applies slow if it bites; burn resin patches to flush them.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Collect borers for warden study jars.
  - Clear a path for mule caravans.

## Milepost Rumor Gatherer
- Type: Humanoid (gossip)
- Where Found: Whisperline Milepost
- Behaviors: Trades rumors for tokens; scribbles notes on driftwood; flees if torches flare.
- Drops: Roadling Trade Token, Ward-Chalk Packet, Whisperline Travel Ration, Murmurstone Charm.
- Threat Notes: Summons Milepost Whisperer if attacked; cut off escape routes.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Deliver fresh chalk to keep the milepost wards aligned.
  - Plant false routes for smugglers.

## Rootsong Cairn Watcher
- Type: Humanoid (warden)
- Where Found: Rootsong Cairn
- Behaviors: Listens for off-key songs; marks offenders; sets torch lines around cairn.
- Drops: Rootsong Listening Horn, Cairnkeeper's Beads, Sapstring Bandage, Ward-Chalk Packet.
- Threat Notes: Calls cairn wisps if provoked; silence or charm to delay.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Escort the watcher through Hollow Sap Grove.
  - Replace damaged torch lines with bridge torches.

## Lowlight Forager
- Type: Humanoid (gatherer)
- Where Found: Lowlight Shroom Beds
- Behaviors: Gathers shrooms carefully; uses tea steam to hide; leaves lantern markers.
- Drops: Lowlight Shroom Tea, Rivergrain Porridge Mix, Sap-Resin Salve, Ward-Chalk Packet.
- Threat Notes: Smoke causes brief blindness; keep distance until it clears.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Protect foragers from hook-runners.
  - Collect glowing caps for Cook Mara.

## Hook-Runner Pup
- Type: Beast
- Where Found: Smuggler's Knot Camp gullies
- Behaviors: Mimics adults by snagging packs; tumbles over ropes; fears loud horns.
- Drops: Smuggler's Knot Hook, Whisperline Travel Ration, Sapstring Bandage, Coiled Line Spool.
- Threat Notes: Bleed on hit; scare with torches to stop pounces.
- Quest Hooks:
  - Capture pups for training with wardens.
  - Remove hooks before they learn to raid caravans.

## Bridge Plank Rat

- Where Found: Under Chorus Bridge planks
- Behaviors: Chews rope; steals rations; squeaks in rhythm with the singer.
- Drops: Whisperline Travel Ration, Sapstring Bandage, Rivergrain Porridge Mix, Bivouac Tent Stakes.

- Quest Hooks:

  - Recover ration crumbs before smugglers poison them.
