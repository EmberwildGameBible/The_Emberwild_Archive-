# Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event

**Origin/Provenance:** Pole-walker chalkboard schedule kept at Lantern Barge Mooring.

**Cultural Tie:** Locals honor Gleam Surges with bell chimes; outsiders see a chance to harvest glowspores while rope guides mutter about “gleam debt.”

**Gameplay Hook:** Repeatable “Gleam Surge Response” where players relight pylons, clear leech swarms, and escort glowspore carriers to safe pools; pays out light-based lures and crafting tallow.

## Event Loop: Gleam Surge Response
- **Start:** Light the first pylon using [Glowspore_Tallow](../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md); timer begins until fog returns.
- **Obstacles:** Leech swarms, cult divers attempting to steal carriers, collapsing rope planks.
- **Rewards:**
  - [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md)
  - [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md)
  - Chance at [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md)
