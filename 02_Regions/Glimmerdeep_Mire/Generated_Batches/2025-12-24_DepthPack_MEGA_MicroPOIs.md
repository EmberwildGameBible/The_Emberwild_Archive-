# Glimmerdeep Microcontent Pack MEGA (DepthPack MEGA)

300 micro-POIs designed to increase moment-to-moment density.

### Glimmerdeep Micro-POI 001: Shiverpine Clearing
- **Vibe:** dangerous in a mundane way, like a place that wants you to slip
- **Gameplay:** 1–2 pulls, a reason to stop, and a reason to leave.
- **Gather / Loot:**
- **Node:** Faded Helm of the Mire (ID 31123) — Mail gear.
- **Node:** Worn Liniment (ID 32765) — Bandage consumable.
- **Node:** Salt-stained Permit (ID 32912) — Ledger document.
- **Enemy Packs:**
  - Wolves (skittish, pack pulls)
  - Bogleeches (slow, stacking debuff)
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Bring back a sample for an alchemist’s field kit.
  - Recover a missing satchel wedged under the stones.
- **In-World Drop:** a blood-smudged roster
- **Design Note:** add one interactable (rope, bell, cache, chalk mark).
