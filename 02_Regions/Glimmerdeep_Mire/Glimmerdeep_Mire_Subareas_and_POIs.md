# Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs

**Origin/Provenance:** Ropeway survey logs from lantern barge guides and ferrymen who map safe pylons.

**Cultural Tie:** Mire pole-walkers mark lantern poles with glowspore smears; Saltmarrow traders note where leeches accept fish guts as tribute.

**Gameplay Hook:** Identifies rare spawns, glowspore harvest points, and ropeway shortcuts; unlocks swamp repeatables and dungeon rumor trails.

## Subareas
- **Lantern Pylon Run:** Series of leaning pylons; rare “Pylon Sentry Sludge” drops [Glowspore_Tallow](../../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md). See also: [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md), [Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub](../../07_Quests/Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md), [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md)
- **Ash Vent Hollows:** Steam vents with cult divers; rare “Ventwatch Diver” drops [Ashen_Vent_Key](../../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md). See also: [Ashen_Cry_Grotto](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md), [Mire_Salvage_Chain](../../07_Quests/Mire_Salvage_Chain.md), [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md)
- **Peatcoil Flats:** Bog mats coil with strangler roots; rare “Peatcoil Strider” yields [Peatcoil_Root_Band](../../08_Items/Gear/Peatcoil_Root_Band.md). See also: [Frostbriar_Reach](../Frostbriar_Reach/Zone_Overview.md), [Old_Road_Circuit](../../07_Quests/Old_Road_Circuit.md), [Peatcoil_Root_Stew_Recipe](../../08_Items/Cooking/Peatcoil_Root_Stew_Recipe.md)
- **Sunken Ledger Wreck:** Half-buried barge with ledger scraps; rare “Ledger Leecher” drops [Sodden_Ledger_Chain](../../09_Artifacts/Sodden_Ledger_Chain.md). See also: [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md), [Glowspore_Trade_Scrip](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Glowspore_Trade_Scrip.md), [Lantern_Barge_Warning](../../09_Artifacts/Lantern_Barge_Warning.md)
- **Gleam-Cup Pools:** Bioluminescent pools; rare “Gleam Eel” used for cooking buffs. See also: [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md), [Frostbite_Pike_Pole](../../08_Items/Fishing/Frostbite_Pike_Pole.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_Settlements](Glimmerdeep_Mire_Settlements.md)

## Points of Interest
- **Ropeway Cranks:** Activate to open shortcuts across the mire.
- **Glowspore Hollows:** Node clusters that respawn during Gleam Surges.
- **Cult Ash Marks:** Burned sigils leading deeper toward Ashen Cry rumors.
