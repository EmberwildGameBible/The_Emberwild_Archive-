# Glimmerdeep_Mire_Settlements

**Origin/Provenance:** Lantern barge manifests and pole-walker rosters collected after the last Gleam Surge season.

**Cultural Tie:** Mirefolk carry glowspore charms; Saltmarrow traders clip wind bells to ropeways to call for ferries.

**Gameplay Hook:** Provides ferries, ropeway unlocks, and vendors selling light-based fishing and cooking supplies.

## Lantern Barge Mooring (Town)
- **Services:** Ferry to ropeway network, glowspore broker, cooking fires immune to mire damp.
- **Economy Notes:** Trades glowspore bundles for lures and recipes like [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md).
- **Vendors:**
  - *Broker Vesha* sells [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md) and [Glowspore_Tallow](../../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md).
  - *Cook Orlen* offers [Peatcoil_Root_Stew_Recipe](../../08_Items/Cooking/Peatcoil_Root_Stew_Recipe.md) and buys [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md).

## Peatcoil Waypoint (Outpost)
- **Services:** Ropeway crank, ventwatch bell that starts [Ventwatch_Bounty_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bounty_Note.md), minor repairs.
- **Economy Notes:** Trades ash vent keys for rope grease; accepts [Ashen_Vent_Key](../../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md) turn-ins for reputation.
- **Vendors:**
  - *Greasehand Mera* sells [Peatcoil_Root_Band](../../08_Items/Gear/Peatcoil_Root_Band.md) and [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md).
