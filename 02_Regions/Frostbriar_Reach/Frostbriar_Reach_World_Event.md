# Frostbriar_Reach_World_Event

**Origin/Provenance:** Timberline Post notice board tracking sled contracts and ash drift sightings.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak crews bet sled times with carved briar tokens; Marrowhook couriers time runs to the beat of slidecaller bells.

**Gameplay Hook:** Repeatable “Slidecaller Escort” event where players guide timber sleds between bells while fending off cult ambushes and avalanches; rewards sled upgrades and dungeon key fragments.

## Event Loop: Slidecaller Escort
- **Start:** Ring a [Slidecaller_Bell_Tab](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Bell_Tab.md) to summon sled and timer.
- **Obstacles:** Wind shear chill, rolling logs, cult sappers planting ash charges.
- **Rewards:**
  - [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md) (rare drop)
  - [Slidecaller_Key_Fragment](../../08_Items/Keys/Slidecaller_Key_Fragment.md) (event currency toward Ashen Cry access)
  - Reputation with Shiverhook Outwatch vendors.
