# Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs

**Origin/Provenance:** Sketches compiled by Windbreak sled drivers after losing timber shipments to storms and cult prowlers.

**Cultural Tie:** Briar camp notches mark safe sled angles; elk handlers leave carved briar knots that only locals read.

**Gameplay Hook:** Guides players to resource veins, rare spawns, and patrol routes; unlocks dailies that rotate by subarea weather.

## Subareas
- **Slidecaller Ridge:** Narrow sled track with avalanche-risk rare “White Howl Matriarch.” Hunters seek her hide for frost-lamellar gear. ## Points of Interest
- **Slidecaller Bells:** Rusted bells warn of sled runs; interacting starts a timed escort for timber bundles.
- **Windbreak Supply Cache:** Restock point for briar-sap salves and cold lamps.
- **Ash Drift Smokes:** Plumes that reveal cult routes during world events.
