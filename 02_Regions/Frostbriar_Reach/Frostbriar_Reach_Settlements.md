# Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements

**Origin/Provenance:** Field notes from Windbreak quartermasters mapping resupply for timber slides and elk trains.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak timber crews run the ledgers; Marrowhook strand-couriers trade saltfish for resin torches.

**Gameplay Hook:** Unlock profession vendors (cold-weather crafts, sled upgrades) and access to repeatable sled escort contracts.

## Timberline Post (Town)
- **Services:** Repair stand, sled craftsman (sells [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md)), herbalist with frost fern contracts.
- **Economy Notes:** Trades timber slips for sled rails and briar-sap salves; hosts a nightly stew pot requiring [Frostbriar_Stag_Shanks](../08_Items/Cooking/Frostbriar_Stag_Shanks.md).
- **Vendors:**
  - *Quartermaster Lerna* sells [Windbreak_Ice_Lantern](../08_Items/Trinkets/Windbreak_Ice_Lantern.md) and [Briar_Sinew_Binding](../08_Items/Crafting/Briar_Sinew_Binding.md).
  - *Cook Brevin* sells recipe [Steeped_Cedar_Stew_Recipe](../08_Items/Cooking/Steeped_Cedar_Stew_Recipe.md).

## Shiverhook Outwatch (Outpost)
- **Services:** Guarded fire ring (removes chill), sled bell relay for escort quests, bounty board that spawns [White_Howl_Rumor_Slip](../../09_Artifacts/White_Howl_Rumor_Slip.md).
- **Economy Notes:** Trades cult trinkets for cold-weather tallow; buys [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md) for reputation.
- **Vendors:**
  - *Scout Grella* offers [Frostbite_Pike_Pole](../08_Items/Fishing/Frostbite_Pike_Pole.md) and [Cold-Iron_Splitshot_Lure](../08_Items/Fishing/Cold-Iron_Splitshot_Lure.md).
