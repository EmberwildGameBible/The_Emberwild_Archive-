300 micro-POIs designed to increase moment-to-moment density.

### Frostbriar Micro-POI 001: Kelp-Wharf Ruins
- **Vibe:** beautiful at first glance, then unsettling
- **Gameplay:** 1–2 pulls, a reason to stop, and a reason to leave.
- **Gather / Loot:**
- **Node:** Salt-stained Pod (ID 32874) — Root ingredient.
- **Node:** Tin-chimed Bandage Roll (ID 30964) — Tea consumable.
- **Node:** Harbor-wet Petal (ID 32972) — Spice ingredient.

  - Wayward Bandits (nets, call reinforcements)
  - Lantern Wraiths (fear, light-sensitive)
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Escort a cart through the area during a storm window.
  - Lay a ward-charm to stop nightly harassment.
- **In-World Drop:** a salted prayer scrap
- **Design Note:** add one interactable (rope, bell, cache, chalk mark).
