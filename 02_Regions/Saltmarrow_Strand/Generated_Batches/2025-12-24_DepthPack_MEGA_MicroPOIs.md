# Saltmarrow Microcontent Pack MEGA (DepthPack MEGA)

300 micro-POIs designed to increase moment-to-moment density.

### Saltmarrow Micro-POI 001: Reed-Hidden Cache
- **Vibe:** beautiful at first glance, then unsettling
- **Gameplay:** 1–2 pulls, a reason to stop, and a reason to leave.
- **Gather / Loot:**
- **Node:** Cedar-scented Biscuit (ID 32571) — Potion consumable.
- **Node:** Iron-banded Ration (ID 30940) — Tea consumable.
- **Node:** Pine-sap Pod (ID 31392) — Insect ingredient.
- **Enemy Packs:**
  - Mirebrood Spitters (poison cones)
  - Kelpback Patrol (shield wall)
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Lay a ward-charm to stop nightly harassment.
  - Bring back a sample for an alchemist’s field kit.
- **In-World Drop:** a salted prayer scrap
- **Design Note:** add one interactable (rope, bell, cache, chalk mark).
