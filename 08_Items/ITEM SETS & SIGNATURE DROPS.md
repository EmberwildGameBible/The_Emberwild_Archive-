# Item Sets & Signature Drops

## Regional & Boss Signatures

### Greatwood
- **Heartwood Vowblade** — Gear (Epic 1H Sword) — Drops from the Ragged King hunt in Greatwood. Short-Lore: vow-marked heartwood blade that hums when hunts are sworn. Special: melee hunters gain bleed cleanse after executing marked targets.
- **Hollowroot Matron's Warding Band** — Gear (Legendary Ring) — Hollowroot Warren, Matron boss chest. Short-Lore: bark-ring pulsing like brood-heartbeats. Special: poison ward that amplifies antidote duration.
- **Sap-Slick Jerkin** — Gear (Rare Leather) — Hunt board and bramble caches. Short-Lore: resin-sealed jerkin for thorn runs. Special: flat bleed resistance and reduced snare duration.
- **Greatwood Trailboots** — Gear (Rare Boots) — Lodge deeds and trail caches. Short-Lore: barkguard soles gripping wet roots. Special: movement speed and stumble immunity on slopes.
- **Warden's Grove Map** — Quest (Rare Map) — Archivist quests, elder stag trophies. Short-Lore: vellum pointing to wardstone pylons. Special: unlocks hidden hunt routes and cache markers.

### Hollowroot Warren
- **Hollowroot Sporesplice** — Ingredient (Rare Fungus) — Spore pods and broodling packs. Short-Lore: braided luminescent spores. Special: antitoxin base for cooking/alchemy.
- **Matron's Broodguard** — Gear (Epic Shield) — Matron fragments and broodguard rares. Short-Lore: chitin bulwark lacquered with sap. Special: high block plus poison-to-heal conversion window.

### Burn-Scar Descent
- **Burn-Scar Chasmbreaker** — Gear (Epic 2H Axe) — Final chest, magma colossus trophies. Short-Lore: slag-edged axe quenched in lava. Special: cleave bursts that leave burning faultlines.
- **Magmaforged Links** — Material (Rare Chain) — Chasm nodes and salamander wardens. Short-Lore: chains still warm from vents. Special: enables fireproof mail crafting and tethering tools.

### Ashen Ridge
- **Ashen Ridge Emberplate** — Gear (Epic Chestplate) — Siege commanders, emberforged caches. Short-Lore: soot-dark plate with ember veins. Special: fire retaliation aura while blocking.
- **Cinderstorm Javelin** — Gear (Epic Spear) — Skirmish bosses and warband coffers. Short-Lore: obsidian shardglass ignites on impact. Special: burn-over-time stacks that escalate on crits.
- **Sootglass Eye** — Curio (Rare Trinket) — Geomancers and faultline caches. Short-Lore: sootglass shard that records tremors. Special: reveals unstable vents and boosts perception.
- **Cragfeather Cape** — Gear (Rare Cloak) — Harpy rares and sky-nest chests. Short-Lore: basalt feathers that shed ash. Special: minor fire ward and glide control.
- **Ashen Spice Rub** — Ingredient (Uncommon Spice) — Camp vendors, cookfire caches. Short-Lore: smoky pepper blend. Special: amplifies fire-aligned meals.

### Frostmarch Tundra
- **Glacierbite Bow** — Gear (Epic Bow) — Alpine hunt rares, icebound world bosses. Short-Lore: frost-laced bow exhaling mist. Special: slows targets on crit and buffs chill damage.
- **Permafrost Greaves** — Gear (Rare Plate Legs) — Warband chests, patrol events. Short-Lore: snowseal-lined greaves. Special: anti-freeze stamina buffer.
- **Tundra Howl Totem** — Curio (Epic Totem) — Howler packs, shrine offerings. Short-Lore: horn totem echoing tundra calls. Special: lure that also grants cold resist.
- **Frostmarch Seal** — Quest (Rare Seal) — Regional questlines, icegate sentinels. Short-Lore: rune-stamped passage marker. Special: opens vaults and amplifies wards.
- **Frostbloom Draught** — Alchemy (Rare Potion) — Crafted/sold by tundra alchemists. Short-Lore: crisp glacier petal brew. Special: cold resist with crit clarity.

### Duskhollow
- **Nightspire Lantern** — Gear (Epic Off-hand) — Nightspire bosses, shade caches. Short-Lore: voidlit lantern staining mist purple. Special: reveals hidden paths and boosts shadow damage.
- **Hollow Veil Hood** — Gear (Epic Hood) — Assassins and veilrunner events. Short-Lore: muffles footfalls, cloaks heat. Special: stealth efficacy and threat drop.
- **Duskfang Dagger** — Gear (Rare Dagger) — Shadowblade elites, lockboxes. Short-Lore: curved dagger that drinks lamplight. Special: shadow bleed with bonus backstab damage.
- **Mistwoven Cloak** — Gear (Rare Cloak) — Mistmoth rares, wardrobe caches. Short-Lore: mist-silk cloak blurring edges. Special: evasion and threat-shed.

### Shattered Verge

- **Veilshard Circuit** — Curio (Epic Relic) — Pylon cores, anomaly custodians. Short-Lore: loop of crystal circuitry. Special: portal stability and spell haste.

## Sets & Synergies

### Small Armor/Weapon Sets
- **Ashen Emberguard (2-piece)** — *Ashen Ridge Emberplate* + *Cragfeather Cape*.
  - Bonus: Minor fire retaliation and glide control when both equipped.
- **Frostmarch Hunter's Promise (3-piece)** — *Glacierbite Bow* + *Permafrost Greaves* + *Tundra Howl Totem*.
  - Bonus: Chill proc chance increased and cold resist feast buff duration +20%.
- **Duskhollow Shadeweave (3-piece)** — *Hollow Veil Hood* + *Duskfang Dagger* + *Mistwoven Cloak*.
  - Bonus: Extended stealth uptime and backstab bonus while cloaked.
- **Verge Sunderer (2-piece)** — *Vergebreaker Maul* + *Veilshard Circuit*.
  - Bonus: Barrier shred potency scales with spell haste.
