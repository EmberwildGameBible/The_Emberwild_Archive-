# EMBERWILD COOKBOOK - FIELD RATIONS & BUFFS

Classic provisioning for dungeon runs and long flights between hubs. Uses the existing 40-ingredient catalog—no new plants or meats.

## QUICK INDEX
- Travel Rations (Lv 10–18) — fast food buffs that don’t break stealth or sprint.
- Dungeon Prep Meals (Lv 20–32) — stamina, resist, and regen meals tuned for group play.
- War Supply Crates — batch-crafted bundles for guilds and warbands.

## TRAVEL RATIONS (LV 10–18)
### Scout’s Pocket Bread (Lv 10)
- **Ingredients:** Hearthgrain Malt, Ambergrain, Everfenn Tea Herb.
- **Method:** Flatbread baked on shields; glazed with tea syrup.
- **Buff:** +6 Stamina for 20 minutes; does not cancel stealth.
- **Notes:** Sold in starting capitals; pairs with Ranger’s Field Breakfast.

### Rider’s Dried Fruit (Lv 12)
- **Ingredients:** Everpear, Emberberry, Sunpepper dust.
- **Method:** Slice, sun-dry, and finish with spice.
- **Buff:** +5 Run Speed (mount and on foot) for 10 minutes.
- **Notes:** Snack category; stackable to 5.

### Trailhand’s Broth Flask (Lv 16)
- **Ingredients:** Mireleaf, Duskbean Legume, Emberkelp Stem.
- **Method:** Slow brew; reduce into concentrated flask.
- **Buff:** +8 Nature Resist and +8 Spirit for 15 minutes.
- **Notes:** Counts as beverage; works while mounted.

## DUNGEON PREP MEALS (LV 20–32)
### Guardian’s Salt Roast (Lv 20)
- **Ingredients:** Boarshank, Breezewood Smoke Salt, Ashseed Pod glaze.
- **Method:** Salt crust then slow roast near coals.
- **Buff:** +14 Stamina and +8 Defense for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Ideal for tanks starting Scarlet-tier wings.

### Arcanist’s Herb Quiche (Lv 24)
- **Ingredients:** Ember Eggs, Ashleaf, Aurora Mint, Brightcorn crust.
- **Method:** Bake in travel tin; infuse mint just before serving.
- **Buff:** +16 Spirit and +12 Spell Power for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Eats count as meal; improves drinking mana regen by 10%.

### Marauder’s Iron Stew (Lv 28)
- **Ingredients:** Cliffgoat Rib, Duskmeat, Canyoncress, Ashbean thickener.
- **Method:** Stew in iron pot with bone marrow reduction.
- **Buff:** +18 Strength and +10 Versatility for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Slightly raises weapon skill gain while active.

### Frostwatcher Chow (Lv 32)
- **Ingredients:** Coldwater Cod, Frostthistle, Driftwheat, Evermushroom.
- **Method:** Creamy chowder with frost spice and mushroom stock.
- **Buff:** +20 Frost Resist and +12 Stamina for 35 minutes.
- **Notes:** Recommended for Stormvein Keystone attunement runs.

## WAR SUPPLY CRATES
- **Crate Types:** Stamina Crate, Spellpower Crate, Resist Crate (choose element), Pet Handler Crate.
- **Contents:** 10 meals + 5 snacks tuned to the crate theme; counts as a trade good.
- **Crafting:** Requires campfire skill 125 and 1x **Warband Packing Straw** (vendor). Bundling takes 2 minutes of channeling.
- **Usage:** Right-click to split contents to your bags; soulbound on pickup to prevent auction flooding.
- **Notes:** Guild quartermasters can reserve crate recipes behind reputation, echoing classic raid prep culture.
