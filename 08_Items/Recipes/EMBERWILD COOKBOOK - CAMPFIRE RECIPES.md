## RECIPE DETAILS

### Fisherman’s Shore Stew (Lv 5)
- **Ingredients:** Creek Trout, Brook Salmon, Emberkelp Stem, Hearthgrain Malt.
- **Method:** Simmer fish scraps with kelp and malt, finish with Sunpepper.
- **Buff:** +3 Versatility for 20 minutes.
- **Notes:** Low fire skill; sold by dockside anglers in starter hubs.

### Wayfarer’s Trail Jerky (Lv 8)
- **Ingredients:** Boarshank, Breezewood Smoke Salt (substitute: Sunpepper), Acreleaf.
- **Method:** Cure overnight, then rack-dry above a low ember fire.
- **Buff:** +4 Attack Power and +5 run speed for 15 minutes.
- **Notes:** Counts as handheld snack; does not interrupt sprint effects.

### Druid’s Root Mash (Lv 10)
- **Ingredients:** Amberroot, Duneroot Seed, Emberflax Stem, Frostthistle.
- **Method:** Roast roots in coals, mash with flax and frost spice.
- **Buff:** +10 Spirit for 25 minutes.
- **Notes:** Herbal-only; favored by healers who avoid meat.

### Ranger’s Field Breakfast (Lv 12)
- **Ingredients:** Ember Eggs (Breeze Egg), Hearthgrain Malt, Everpear slices.
- **Method:** Fry eggs in Briarfat, glaze with pear reduction.
- **Buff:** +6 Stamina and +6 Agility for 20 minutes.
- **Notes:** Counts as a breakfast; increases pet loyalty gain slightly.

### Swamper’s Mire Gumbo (Lv 14)
- **Ingredients:** Crawler Meat, Brine Bass, Mireleaf (use Duskleaf), Bonefang stock.
- **Method:** Slow simmer with mire herbs; add Ashbean to thicken.
- **Buff:** +8 Nature Resist and +5 Stamina for 25 minutes.
- **Notes:** Reduces disease effect duration by 10% while active.

### Miner’s Ember Pie (Lv 16)
- **Ingredients:** Ashwing Cut, Ashseed Pod, Dropseed crust, Firecap (Sunpepper variant).
- **Method:** Bake sealed pies in shovel coals; vent with ember sigil.
- **Buff:** +10 Fire Resist and +6 Strength for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** High heat skill; stations in mining camps or dwarven forges.

### Outrider’s Skillet Hash (Lv 18)
- **Ingredients:** Wildfowl Scraps (Alderfenn Fat + Breeze Egg), Brightcorn, Canyoncress.
- **Method:** Pan fry with rendered fat until crispy; dust with Sunpepper.
- **Buff:** +10 Critical Strike rating for 25 minutes.
- **Notes:** Counts as a snack; does not stack with battlefield rations.

### Pilgrim’s Hearth Loaf (Lv 20)
- **Ingredients:** Ambergrain, Duskbean Legume, Hearthgrain Malt, Everfenn Tea Herb.
- **Method:** Slow-rise loaf baked in lidded pot; brush with tea syrup.
- **Buff:** +12 Spirit and +8 Stamina for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Increases out-of-combat mana regen by 15% during effect.

### Sentinel’s Smoke Chowder (Lv 22)
- **Ingredients:** Coldwater Cod, Driftwheat, Fennel Frond (Emberkelp Stem alt), Frostthistle.
- **Method:** Smoke cod on cedar, fold into creamy chowder with frost spice.
- **Buff:** +12 Frost Resist and +8 Stamina for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Cold-zone specialty; sold in alpine watchposts.

### Beastmaster’s Stockpot (Lv 24)
- **Ingredients:** Wild Elk Bones (Arbor Elk Meat), Evermushroom (substitute Everfenn Greens), Azurefin.
- **Method:** Long-simmer bone broth with kelp and river herbs.
- **Buff:** +15 Pet Health and +10 Pet Damage for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Applies to summoned companions; stacks with handler talents.

### Vanguard Roast (Lv 26)
- **Ingredients:** Cliffgoat Rib, Duskmeat, Sunpepper, Hearthgrain Malt glaze.
- **Method:** Spit-roast over oak coals; baste with malt reduction.
- **Buff:** +15 Defense and +12 Stamina for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Threat generation increased by 8% while the meal is active.

### Stormcall Soup (Lv 28)
- **Ingredients:** Azurefin, Aerobloom, Emberberry, Frostthistle.
- **Method:** Brew kelp-fish stock, steep with sky herbs and frost spice.
- **Buff:** +15 Spell Power and +10 Frost/Storm crit for 30 minutes.
- **Notes:** Popular with druids and shamans; does not stack with mage-specific conjured food.
