# Glowspore_Trade_Scrip

**Origin/Provenance:** Waterproofed ledger slips stamped with glowspore wax, issued by Lantern Barge brokers.

**Cultural Tie:** Mirefolk use scrip to settle “gleam debt”; Saltmarrow traders distrust it but accept bundles during surges.

**Gameplay Hook:** Currency token used in Glimmerdeep vendor purchases and some quest turn-ins; drops from Ashen Cry Hierarch cache.
