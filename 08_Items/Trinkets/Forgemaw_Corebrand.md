# Forgemaw_Corebrand

**Origin/Provenance:** Branded medallion pressed from cooled Forgemaw bile and ore dust.

**Cultural Tie:** Hollowroot smith apprentices wear copies to prove they’ve tempered steel in beast heat.

**Gameplay Hook:** Trinket that stores fire charges and expends them to buff allies’ weapon heat; defeating Forgemaw without killing marked adds grants it and updates [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
