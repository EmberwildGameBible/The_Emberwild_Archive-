# Silent Bell Nightwarden Codex (Item ID: 50510)

## Origin/Provenance
Compiled by covert wardens who watch for Silent Bell activity beneath the [Emberford Marketwalk](../../02_Regions/Emberford_Marketwalk/Zone_Index.md). The vellum bears ink dusted with ground bell metal.
