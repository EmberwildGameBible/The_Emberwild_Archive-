# Ashen Cry Emberfall Circlet (Item ID: 50507)

## Origin/Provenance
Forged from cooled ashglass inside the [Ashen Cry Grotto](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md) altar after a Slidebreaker cleared the vents.

## Cultural Tie
Slidecaller aspirants wear it when reciting breach-sealing rites. The circlet’s sigils echo the markers used in the [Ashen Cry Grotto quests](../../07_Quests/Dungeon/Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests.md).

## Gameplay Hook
Legendary headband that halves fire damage while channeling and adds a small heal when you extinguish a hazard. Earned from the Ashen Cry Hierarch per the updated loot table and required to close the "Emberfall" chapter in [Legendary Chains — Pack Emberwild](../../07_Quests/Generated_Batches/2025-12-24_Legendary_Chains_Pack.md).
