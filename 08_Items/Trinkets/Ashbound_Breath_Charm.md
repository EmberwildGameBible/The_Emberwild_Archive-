# Ashbound_Breath_Charm

**Origin/Provenance:** Smoky glass bead strung on briar sinew, looted from Ash Drift envoys.

**Cultural Tie:** Cultists inhale through the bead before chanting; Windbreak scouts trade them in for hazard pay proof.

**Gameplay Hook:** Trinket granting brief fire resistance and needed as a turn-in for Frostbriar and Glimmerdeep quests.
