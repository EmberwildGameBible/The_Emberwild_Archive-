# Siren_Throatspore_Flute

**Origin/Provenance:** Bone flute drilled from a Siren spine, its holes packed with calming spores.

**Cultural Tie:** Hollowroot herders use similar whistles to settle rampaging bog bulls.

**Gameplay Hook:** Trinket granting haste to interrupt chains and a group calm aura after cone shrieks; dropping from Vent-Siren and appended to [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
