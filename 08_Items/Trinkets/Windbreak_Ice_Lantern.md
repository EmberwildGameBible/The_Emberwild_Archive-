# Windbreak_Ice_Lantern

**Origin/Provenance:** Crafted by Timberline lanternwrights using glowspore oil trapped in frostglass shells.

**Cultural Tie:** Carried by Windbreak scouts as proof against whiteout nights; Marrowhook couriers rent them for night slides.

**Gameplay Hook:** Trinket providing brief chill resistance and reveals ashbound tracks during world events and dungeon entries.
