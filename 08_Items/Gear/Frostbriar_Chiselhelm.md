# Frostbriar Chiselhelm

## Origin/Provenance
A helm hammered to hold replaceable visor plates for frost debris. A chipped note inside the [Frostbriar Reach zone overview](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Zone_Overview.md) lists the first wearer as a miner who dug past a sealed ice gate.

## Cultural Tie
Helms of this style hang above workshop doors to honor forebears who carved the tunnels. The etched iceflowers match motifs found in [Frostbriar Reach POIs](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs.md), tying the craft to place.

## Gameplay Hook
Epic helmet that reduces chip waste for stonecutters and reveals frost seams on the minimap. Adds cold resistance in [Frostbriar travel segments](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Zone_Index.md) and grants smiths extra proc chance on chilled alloys.
