# Hierarchs_Ashen_Tide_Stole

**Origin/Provenance:** Stole stitched from ashglass-threaded linen worn by the Ashen Cry Hierarch during final vent rites.

**Cultural Tie:** Cult chanters believe the fringe keeps smoke spirits docile; Windbreak clergy repurpose it as a warning pennant over rope bridges.

**Gameplay Hook:** Shoulder item that grants fire-to-frost damage conversion while standing in ashfall; wearing it while carrying vent keys adds a group haste pulse. Looting it records [Slidecaller_Field_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Field_Note.md).
