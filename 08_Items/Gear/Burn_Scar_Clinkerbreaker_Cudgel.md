# Burn Scar Clinkerbreaker Cudgel (Item ID: 50509)

## Origin/Provenance
Hammered from slag nodules found along the [Burn Scar Descent](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Burn_Scar_Descent/Dungeon_Overview.md) ramps, wrapped in vent-charred leather.

## Cultural Tie
Used by vent crews to smash clinker that blocks the descent lifts. Its handle is carved with the same tally marks noted in the Burn Scar freight permits.

## Gameplay Hook
Legendary mace that applies stacking sunder to armored targets and grants fire absorb while stationary.
