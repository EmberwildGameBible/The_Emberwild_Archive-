# Shardbound_Oath_Breastplate

**Origin/Provenance:** Breastplate inlaid with mirrored ribs, hammered in the Crypt anvil room after the Oathmirror trial.

**Cultural Tie:** Riverwatch duelists swap tales about polishing the mirrors with ember salt to catch lies.

**Gameplay Hook:** Chest gear granting stacking ward when blocking spells and minor party reflect; second Shardbound Covenant piece linked to [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
