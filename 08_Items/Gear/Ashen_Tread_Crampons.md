# Ashen_Tread_Crampons

**Origin/Provenance:** Spiked crampons hammered from sled-runner scrap by Timberline smiths responding to ash-ice slides in the grotto.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak scouts etch Shiverhook tally marks into the plates for every bridge rescue.

**Gameplay Hook:** Boot slot with reduced slide chance and bonus grip on ash-slick ropes; final piece of the Slidecaller Relay set. Looting them updates [Slidecaller_Field_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Field_Note.md).
