# Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves

**Origin/Provenance:** Rope-handling gloves treated with glowspore resin, made by Glimmerdeep rope smiths.

**Cultural Tie:** Pole-walkers swap stories about “grease burns” when the resin flares; Saltmarrow sailors buy them for wet rigging.

**Gameplay Hook:** Gear piece giving bonus to ropeway interactions and reducing fall risk on rope bridges; drops in Ashen Cry Grotto.
