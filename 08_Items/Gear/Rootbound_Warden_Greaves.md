# Rootbound_Warden_Greaves

**Origin/Provenance:** Greaves hammered from spore-crusted vent plates and lined with mire leather for Hollowroot patrols.

**Cultural Tie:** Glimmerdeep anglers strap eel scales over the shins, claiming it keeps leeches from biting through.

**Gameplay Hook:** Leg gear reducing slow effects from mud and boosting sprint after dodging cone screams; final Rootbound Mycelium set piece tied to [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
