# Peatcoil_Root_Band

**Origin/Provenance:** Wrist wrap woven from peatcoil roots and lined with waxed cloth, sold at Peatcoil Waypoint.

**Cultural Tie:** Pole-walkers wear it to keep blood warm on rope runs; Saltmarrow ferriers decorate theirs with fishbone pins.

**Gameplay Hook:** Minor stamina gear for mid-level players; bonus grip for ropeway interactions and repeatable quests.
