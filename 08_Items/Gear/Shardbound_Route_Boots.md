# Shardbound_Route_Boots

**Origin/Provenance:** Boots studded with shatterglass shards and vent leather to grip the Crypt’s slick halls.

**Cultural Tie:** Riverwatch runners sew prayer ribbons into the laces so the Prophet’s whispers “slide off.”

**Gameplay Hook:** Footwear that resists knockbacks from fissures and grants a sprint when reflecting spells; final Shardbound Covenant piece tied to [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
