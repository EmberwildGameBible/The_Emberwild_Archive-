# Cinder_Wyrmling_Glasswing_Bow

**Origin/Provenance:** Bow strung with furnace hair and limbs lined in translucent glass scales from the Crypt wyrmling.

**Cultural Tie:** Riverwatch hunters compare its shimmer to the Shiverhook skyline after a storm.

**Gameplay Hook:** Ranged weapon that gains heat stacks when you kite around vent circles and fires a reflecting arrow on full stacks; awarded for Cinder Wyrmling two-shot kills and logged in [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
