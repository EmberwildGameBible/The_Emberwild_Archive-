# Slagrunner_Heatbrace

**Origin/Provenance:** Belt forged from slag chain links and insulated with ventwyrm scale scraps by Emberford smiths.

**Cultural Tie:** Freight-runners knot ember-red cords on the buckle for every safe haul through Burn Scar fissures.

**Gameplay Hook:** Waist item granting heat soak reduction and bonus carry speed for slag charges; second piece of the Slagrunner Signal set, advancing [Slagrunner_Log](../../09_Artifacts/Slagrunner_Log.md).
