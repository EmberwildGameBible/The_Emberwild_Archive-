# Glowspore_Signal_Hood

**Origin/Provenance:** Hood woven from frostbriar bark and coated in glowspore lacquer by Glimmerdeep pole-walkers who scouted Ashen Cry Grotto.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak couriers bead ashglass along the hem to signal safe rope paths back to Timberline Post.

**Gameplay Hook:** Head slot item granting dim-light vision and vent-smoke resist; part of the Slidecaller Relay set. Equipping it records [Slidecaller_Field_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Field_Note.md) progress.
