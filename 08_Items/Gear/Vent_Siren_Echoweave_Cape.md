# Vent_Siren_Echoweave_Cape

**Origin/Provenance:** Cape woven from dampened mycelium fibers and Siren membrane scraps recovered from the vent chamber.

**Cultural Tie:** Glimmerdeep couriers embroider wave patterns along the hem to mark cleared sound tunnels.

**Gameplay Hook:** Back piece that reduces cone damage and amplifies ally shouts; wearing it during Vent-Siren unlocks bonus loot and notes [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
