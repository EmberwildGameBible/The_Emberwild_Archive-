# Greatwood Grovekeeper's Crown (Item ID: 50504)

## Origin/Provenance
Braided antler and ironwood circlet blessed at the [Greatwood heart lodges](../../02_Regions/Greatwood/Greatwood.md) after a season of riverwatch patrols.

## Cultural Tie
Presented by grovewardens to captains who keep the ferry commons safe from briar spirits. The crown’s bark inlay repeats patterns carved on the [Greatwood Grovebound Compass](../Keys/Greatwood_Grovebound_Compass.md).

## Gameplay Hook
Legendary helm that adds a stacking nature shield when you block, plus +5% gather yield on rare herbs. Central to the "Crown of Rivers" chain within [Legendary Chains — Pack Emberwild](../../07_Quests/Generated_Batches/2025-12-24_Legendary_Chains_Pack.md) and validated by grove permit slips.
