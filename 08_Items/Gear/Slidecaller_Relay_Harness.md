# Slidecaller_Relay_Harness

**Origin/Provenance:** Rope-padded harness issued to Shiverhook bridge crews after the ash vent collapse reports.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak sledders stitch frost-briar knots along the straps so pole-walkers can clip lantern lines mid-sprint.

**Gameplay Hook:** Chest gear that grants sprint speed and rope-interact stability in Ashen Cry Grotto; completes the Slidecaller Relay set. Acquiring it logs [Slidecaller_Field_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Field_Note.md).
