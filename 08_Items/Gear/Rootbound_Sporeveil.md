# Rootbound_Sporeveil

**Origin/Provenance:** Veil stitched from Hollowroot shelf fungus and reflective glass chips scavenged after the Glass Sentinel fight.

**Cultural Tie:** Mire scouts lace it with Riverwatch prayer knots to keep spores from settling in their hair.

**Gameplay Hook:** Head piece granting blind resist and spore cloud detection; first piece of the Rootbound Mycelium set that logs [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
