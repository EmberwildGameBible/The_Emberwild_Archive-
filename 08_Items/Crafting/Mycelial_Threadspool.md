# Mycelial_Threadspool

**Origin/Provenance:** Spools of living thread cultivated on Hollowroot support beams and harvested by Riverwatch stitchers.

**Cultural Tie:** Hollowroot weavers trade spools for mirror shards to keep the fungi calm during storm seasons.

**Gameplay Hook:** Profession material enabling tailored resist wraps and engineering dampeners; turning in stacks unlocks extra rolls on Glass Sentinel and Forgemaw while updating [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
