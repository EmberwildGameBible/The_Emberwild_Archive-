# Glowspore_Sledge_Kit

**Origin/Provenance:** Bundle of resin-coated ropes, sled runners, and glowspore pitch stitched together for emergency bridge resets in Ashen Cry.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak carpenters swap kit pieces with Glimmerdeep pole-walkers in exchange for smoke-scouting notes.

**Gameplay Hook:** Profession kit used by engineering and woodworking to rebuild rope anchors mid-dungeon; turning in components grants bonus Slidebreaker loot and advances [Slidecaller_Field_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Field_Note.md).
