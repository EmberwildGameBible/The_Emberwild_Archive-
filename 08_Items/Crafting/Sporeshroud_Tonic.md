# Sporeshroud_Tonic

**Origin/Provenance:** Brew made from mycelial dew and chill spores collected near Vent-Siren alcoves.

**Cultural Tie:** Hollowroot herbwitches bottle it in waxed gourds, warning outsiders to sip slowly.

**Gameplay Hook:** Consumable granting nature resist and brief silence immunity; boosts drop rate of Siren-linked trinkets when active. Drinking it logs [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
