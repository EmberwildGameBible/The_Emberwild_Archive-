# Glowspore_Tallow

**Origin/Provenance:** Rendered from glowspore blooms collected during Gleam Surges.

**Cultural Tie:** Pole-walkers smear it on boots and ropes; Saltmarrow fishers buy tins to light night nets.

**Gameplay Hook:** Crafting reagent for lures and lantern gear; needed to start Gleam Surge Response event.
