# Prismthread_Reinforcement

**Origin/Provenance:** Spools of glimmer thread soaked in reflective resin from Shatterglass prism vats.

**Cultural Tie:** Riverwatch tailors weave it into oath cloaks; Glimmerdeep crafters trade it for saltlight pearls.

**Gameplay Hook:** Material for enchanting reflect gear and crafting resist wraps; turning in bundles grants extra vault rolls and advances [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
