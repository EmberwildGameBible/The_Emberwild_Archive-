# Kelpback Binding Straps

Leather bindings reinforced with dried kelp fibers.

- Origin/Provenance: Tanned at Hookbite Lookout using kelp lifted from Kelpback Shallows.
- Cultural tie: Given to couriers who complete the first dusk loop without dropping a bundle.
- Gameplay hook: Component for stabilizing poles and backpacks; required turn-in for [Windbreak Bounty Board](../../07_Quests/Windbreak_Bounty_Board.md) tasks.
