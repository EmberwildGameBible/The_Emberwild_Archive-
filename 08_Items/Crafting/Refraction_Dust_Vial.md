# Refraction_Dust_Vial

**Origin/Provenance:** Vial of powdered shatterglass swept from Crypt floors and sieved by Riverwatch alchemists.

**Cultural Tie:** Used by oath-takers to dust helmets before duels, claiming it wards off lies.

**Gameplay Hook:** Consumable applying short spell reflect and fire resist; using it before Cinder Wyrmling increases signature drop chance and logs [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
