# Slagfuse_Filament

**Origin/Provenance:** Thread of resinous slag drawn from cooled vent seams in Burn Scar Descent.

**Cultural Tie:** Emberford weavers braid it into belts for heat warding; Glimmerdeep fishers buy strands to weight nets.

**Gameplay Hook:** Profession material used for engineering heat baffles and tailoring resist wraps; turning in ten to the freight foreman grants a bonus vent flare loot roll and advances [Slagrunner_Log](../../09_Artifacts/Slagrunner_Log.md).
