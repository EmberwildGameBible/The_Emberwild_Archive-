# Ashen_Cry_Lantern_Pike

**Origin/Provenance:** Pole seized from the Ashen Cry Hierarch, fitted with glowglass guides and ash-burnished grips.

**Cultural Tie:** Cultists wielded it as a ritual staff; Windbreak anglers repurpose it for vent fishing and boast about “borrowing the Hierarch’s light.”

**Gameplay Hook:** Rare fishing pole granting bonuses in ash vents and dungeon pools; prerequisite for a future attunement chain.
