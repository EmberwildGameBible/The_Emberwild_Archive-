# Brineglass Pickling Stone

A palm-sized salt crystal used as a weight to cure fish.

- Origin/Provenance: Cut from Brineglass Flats slabs and traded at Tidecall Piers.
- Cultural tie: Families etch initials into the stone, passing it down with oral brining songs.
- Gameplay hook: Unlocks a pickling bonus in cooking, improving [Salt-Sear Chowder](./Salt-Sear_Chowder.md) yields during town events.
