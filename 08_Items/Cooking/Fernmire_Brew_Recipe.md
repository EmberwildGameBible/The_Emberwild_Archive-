# Fernmire_Brew_Recipe

**Origin/Provenance:** Passed between herbalists who steep frost ferns and leech sacs gathered in Fernmire Flats.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak scouts drink it before cold patrols; mirefolk claim it keeps “gleam debt” at bay when crossing glowspore pools.

**Gameplay Hook:** Recipe crafting a tonic that reduces poison ticks and boosts chill resistance; requested by Frostbriar quests.
