# Ashen_Vent_Key

**Origin/Provenance:** Brass key etched with ash sigils, claimed from Ventwatch Divers and the Ashen Cry Hierarch.

**Cultural Tie:** Cult divers wear it as proof of vent mastery; pole-walkers see it as ransom to open sealed ropeway caches.

**Gameplay Hook:** Unlocks ash vent chests in Glimmerdeep and shortens Ashen Cry Grotto attunement; requested by questgivers for reputation.
