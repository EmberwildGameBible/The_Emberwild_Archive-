# Slidecaller_Key_Fragment

**Origin/Provenance:** Broken bell key from cult saboteurs in Slidecaller Ridge.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak scouts keep fragments to reforge gate keys; cultists wear them as necklaces to brag about sabotaged sleds.

**Gameplay Hook:** Currency piece for Slidecaller Escort events and Ashen Cry dungeon access quests; drops from Slidebreaker Tandem.
