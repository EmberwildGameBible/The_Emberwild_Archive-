# Item Taxonomy — Global Overview

This overview defines shared item families, their purposes in play, and how they intersect with Emberwild professions and systems. Rarity guidance reflects the Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic spread already in use, with occasional Ultra-Rare notes for exceptional drops.

## Ingredients
- **Fruits & Vegetables** — Fresh forageables and staples for Cooking and Foraging recipes; mostly Common/Uncommon with Rare festival produce.
- **Meats & Fish** — Core proteins from Hunting and Fishing that fuel Cooking, Alchemy reagents, and rations; typically Common/Uncommon with rare Trophy cuts.
- **Herbs, Flowers, and Spices** — Fine flavor and alchemical notes gathered by Herbalism/Foraging; spreads from Common to Rare for potent petals and roots.
- **Fungus & Mosses** — Cave and swamp finds for Cooking buffs and Alchemy; skew Uncommon/Rare due to niche habitats.
- **Liquids, Grains, Breads, Sweeteners** — Pressed oils, brews, and staples crafted by Cooking or traded; Common to Rare based on age and purity.
- **Misc Additives** — Salts, minerals, dusts, and crafted seasonings for Cooking/Alchemy; Common/Uncommon with Rare catalytic salts.

## Gear
- **Weapons** — Swords, axes, bows, staves, and polearms forged by Smithing/Bowyer and found on dungeon bosses or world drops; Uncommon to Epic.
- **Armor** — Cloth, leather, mail, and plate crafted by Tailoring/Leathersmithing/Armorsmithing; Common leveling pieces up to Epic raid sets.
- **Jewelry & Trinkets** — Rings, necklaces, charms, and curios that deliver on-use effects; span Uncommon to Epic with Ultra-Rare chase pieces.
- **Off-hands & Shields** — Tomes, orbs, bucklers, and greatshields from crafting and dungeons; Uncommon to Rare baseline with Epic anchors.
- **Tools & Kits** — Profession power tools (e.g., hammers, rods) used to gather and craft; Common/Uncommon with Rare mastery kits.

## Materials
- **Ores & Bars** — Metal nodes, ingots, and alloys mined for Smithing/Bowyer; Common ores to Epic alloys tied to late-game nodes.
- **Woods & Fibers** — Logged timbers, treated shafts, cloth bolts, and leathers for Bowyer, Tailoring, and Leathersmithing; Common/Uncommon staples with Rare hardwoods and cured hides.
- **Monster Parts** — Claws, glands, scales, and antlers for Alchemy, Smithing adornments, and trophies; Uncommon to Epic based on creature difficulty.
- **Essences & Crystals** — Arcane stones, ember shards, and anima residues for Enchantments and Alchemy; Rare/Epic, sometimes Ultra-Rare.
- **Alchemical Components** — Catalysts, powders, and distillates that bridge Ingredients and Materials; Uncommon to Rare.

## Quest & World Objects
- **Quest Items** — Single-use narrative objects; rarity rarely shown but aligns to quest tier (Common to Rare).
- **Curios & Documents** — Notes, maps, and keepsakes that expand lore, often from exploration; Common/Uncommon with Rare lore anchors.
- **Trophies** — Display-worthy drops from bosses and notable beasts; Rare/Epic and tied to housing or prestige quests.
- **Keys & Seals** — Unlock delves, vaults, or events; Uncommon to Rare with Epic raid locks.
- **World Props** — Interactable items placed in the world for ambient storytelling; typically Common.

## Profession Tools
- **Fishing Gear** — Rods, tackle, bait boxes, and nets for Fishing; Common to Rare with named rods as Epic rewards.
- **Cooking Utensils** — Pans, knives, ladles, and spice racks boosting Cooking; Common/Uncommon with Rare chef kits.
- **Hunting & Trapping Tools** — Traps, calls, skinning knives for Hunting/Leathersmithing; Common to Rare.
- **Smithing Implements** — Hammers, tongs, bellows, and grindstones for Weaponsmithing/Armorsmithing; Common/Uncommon with Rare mastercraft sets.
- **Tailoring & Fletching Kits** — Needles, looms, fletching jigs, and dyes for Tailoring/Bowyer; Common/Uncommon with Rare artisan sets.
- **Alchemy Apparatus** — Mortars, alembics, and distillers for Alchemy; Uncommon to Rare with Epic ritual kits.

## Rarity & Usage Notes
- **Common** — Readily found while leveling, in basic nodes, or via vendors; fuels early crafts and basic buffs.
- **Uncommon** — Requires targeted gathering, light dungeons, or specialized nodes; anchors mid-tier crafts.
- **Rare** — Dungeon bosses, rare node spawns, or faction vendors; used for notable gear, advanced recipes, and profession breakthroughs.
- **Epic** — Raid drops, legendary beasts, or story gates; limited-use chase items for best-in-slot crafts or prestige trophies.

Each item family links to the professions and systems that craft, consume, or drop them, and the taxonomy should be used when tagging new entries in the master list, profession files, and data tables.
