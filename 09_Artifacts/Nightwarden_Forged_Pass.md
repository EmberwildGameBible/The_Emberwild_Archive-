# Nightwarden Forged Pass

Forgery stamped with bell-dusted ink.
- Grants temporary immunity from patrol searches while carrying the [Nightwarden Codex](../08_Items/Trinkets/Silent_Bell_Nightwarden_Codex.md) during the "Nightwarden" chain in [Legendary Chains — Pack Emberwild](../07_Quests/Generated_Batches/2025-12-24_Legendary_Chains_Pack.md).
- Notes the Silent Bell enforcer must be defeated during the moonless phase per [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) to earn the codex.
- Scribbled signature: "Do not trust the ferryman." — Agent Hyss.
