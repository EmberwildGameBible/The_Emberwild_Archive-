# Glimmerdeep Chasm Salvage Permit

Ink-wet permit stamped by Broker Vesha’s seal.
- Grants right to recover the [Chasm-Singer Chimes](../08_Items/Trinkets/Glimmerdeep_Chasm-Singer_Chimes.md) after clearing the ventboss listed in [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md).
- Required handoff step for the "Chasm Singer" segment in [Legendary Chains — Pack Emberwild](../07_Quests/Generated_Batches/2025-12-24_Legendary_Chains_Pack.md).
- Location hint: ropeway pylon three, marked with glow-twine.
