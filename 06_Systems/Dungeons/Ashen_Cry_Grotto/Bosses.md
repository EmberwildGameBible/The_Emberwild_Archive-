# Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Bosses

**Origin/Provenance:** Compiled from Shiverhook scouting runs and cultist captured notes.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak scouts name bosses after the sounds they make in the vents; cultists use honorifics tied to smoke and ash.

**Gameplay Hook:** Provides mechanics callouts and loot themes for the dungeon.

1. **Frostbite Warden** — Briar golem frozen in place; lashes players who linger on brittle bridges. Drops [Windbreak_Ice_Lantern](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Windbreak_Ice_Lantern.md).
2. **Embermouth Vicar** — Cult chanter who heats vents; players douse fire with meltwater buckets. Drops [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md).
3. **Cinderline Skitter** — Massive centipede that tunnels between ash vents; leaves burning trails cleared by rope crank levers. Drops [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md).
4. **Slidebreaker Tandem** — Pair of cult sled-saboteurs using rolling logs; interrupt to prevent bridge collapse. Drops [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md).
5. **Gleamdrain Leechking** — Feeds on glowspore pools; purges buffs unless players light pylons. Drops [Glowspore_Tallow](../../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md).
6. **Ashen Cry Hierarch** — Final boss chanting in ashfall; rotates between smoke phases and frost shocks. Chance to drop [Ashen_Vent_Key](../../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md) and [Ashen_Cry_Lantern_Pike](../../08_Items/Fishing/Ashen_Cry_Lantern_Pike.md).
