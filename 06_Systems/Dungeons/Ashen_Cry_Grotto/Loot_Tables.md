# Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Loot_Tables

**Origin/Provenance:** Cataloged by Timberline Post quartermasters comparing early runs.

**Cultural Tie:** Loot features rope-and-lantern craftsmanship favored by Windbreak sledders and glowspore-treated gear traded with Glimmerdeep pole-walkers.

**Gameplay Hook:** Shows repeatable drop targets for profession mats, lures, and mid-tier gear. The mini-set ties directly into bridge rescue quests.
