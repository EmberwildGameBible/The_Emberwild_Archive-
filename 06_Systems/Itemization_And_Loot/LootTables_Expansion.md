# Loot Tables Expansion — Micro-Locations and Contracts

Anchoring new ingredients, gear, and recipes to specific sources to avoid floating data.

## Cinderfen Hot Springs Terraces
- Trash: Ash Stalkers drop ING-001 through ING-050 at 18% combined chance; small coin purses.
- Elites: Heatwatch Sentinels drop GEAR-010, GEAR-034, and REC-015 notes; bonus Ember Sap Bark.
- Boss: Steamwatcher Obelisk chest guarantees one Epic item from GEAR-100–GEAR-120 pool and 2 random REC scrolls.

## Glassbloom Run Terraces
- Trash: Shale Striders drop ING-051–ING-120, with 5% chance at reflective quartz clusters used by enchanters.
- Elites: Veilbound Sentinels drop GEAR-121–GEAR-150 and REC-045/REC-046 lens patterns.
- Boss: Shatterbelly Colossus drops GEAR-151–GEAR-165 plus 3x ING-300+ bundles.

## Morrowpine Tangle Groves
- Trash: Canopy Broodlings drop ING-121–ING-200 and occasional feathered skins.
- Elites: Tangle Wardens drop GEAR-166–GEAR-210 and REC-080 jerky chains; higher hide yield for leatherworkers.
- Boss: Hollowheart Matriarch drops GEAR-211–GEAR-230 and guaranteed REC-095.

## Veilreef Cavern Pools
- Trash: Tide eels drop ING-201–ING-260 and pearl shards.
- Elites: Glowbrood Matriarchs drop GEAR-231–GEAR-260; recipes REC-120–REC-135 tied to alchemy infusions.
- Boss: Tide-locked cache awards one random Epic REC and a roll on ING-500+ bioluminescent stock.

## Starfall Orchard Shelves
- Trash: Orchard Worgs drop ING-261–ING-320 and small game meat.
- Elites: Meteorfall Wardens drop GEAR-261–GEAR-310 with fire-aligned affixes; REC-150–REC-165 cider presses.
- Boss: Comet Husk Core drops GEAR-311–GEAR-330 plus Starfruit contracts for repeatables.

## Umberdeep Quarry Galleries
- Trash: Quarry Brutes drop ING-321–ING-380 and raw ore chips.
- Elites: Lantern Foremen drop GEAR-331–GEAR-370 and REC-180–REC-195 forging routes.
- Boss: Smuggler's Cart Cache rolls two items from GEAR-371–GEAR-390 and one bundle of ING-600+.

## Frostborne Steppe Totems
- Trash: Rime Stalkers drop ING-381–ING-440 and frozen hides.
- Elites: Frostbound Wardens drop GEAR-391–GEAR-430 and REC-210–REC-225 frostbite cures.
- Boss: Totem Warden chest drops GEAR-431–GEAR-450 and 1x random ingredient bundle from ING-700+.

## Gloamhollow Fissure Rifts
- Trash: Gloam Terrors drop ING-441–ING-500 and Gloom Dust satchels.
- Elites: Shadow Bursters drop GEAR-451–GEAR-500 and REC-240–REC-255 shadow protections.
- Boss: Rift Echo event drops GEAR-501–GEAR-520 plus guaranteed quest item for QST-020 follow-up.

## Contract & Vendor Integrations
- Bounty Boards in each micro-location sell weekly caches: 5x assorted ING from local ranges and 1x REC from nearby pool.
- Lodgekeeper Merra adds rotating stock of REC-260–REC-300 and curated GEAR-521–GEAR-540 items after players finish QST-010.
- Trailwarden Quartermaster Joren offers profession kits bundling 10x region-specific ING and repair tokens for 15 silver.
