# Loot Tables Expansion — Micro-Locations and Contracts

Anchoring new ingredients, gear, and recipes to specific sources to avoid floating data.

## Contract & Vendor Integrations
- Bounty Boards in each micro-location sell weekly caches: 5x assorted ING from the local drops above and 1x REC aligned to nearby profession pools (e.g., REC-120 in Veilreef, REC-210 in Frostborne).
- Lodgekeeper Merra adds rotating stock of [REC-260–REC-300] plus curated items from [GEAR-021 — Umberkeep Charm 30], [GEAR-022 — Veilbound Totem 31], and [GEAR-023 — Warden's Girdle 32] after players finish QST-010.
- Trailwarden Quartermaster Joren offers profession kits bundling 10x region-specific ING (matching the drops listed per zone) and repair tokens for 15 silver.
