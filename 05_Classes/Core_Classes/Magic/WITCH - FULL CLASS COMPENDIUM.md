# 1.1 CLASS FANTASY — THE HEXBINDER

The Witch is Emberwild’s pure pressure caster, a medium-range controller who weaponizes curses, hex marks, draining fields, and escalating ritual magic.

Not a necromancer.

Not a summoner.

Not a shadow priest.

Not a warlock.

The Witch is a hex-weaver who manipulates unseen tether-lines, tracking enemies and amplifying damage through Marks, Ritual Circles, and Binding Links.

### Visual Fantasy

- black-green hex sigils
- drifting candle-flame embers
- ash-fall sparks
- runes that flip and invert
- tether lines of faint purple-gray energy
- curses blooming like ink in water
- ritual circles that shimmer and distort

### Identity Pillars

- hex pressure over raw burst
- ritual preparation over spam
- curse stacking over nukes
- tether links over pets
- positional control over mobility
- slow escalation over fast rotations

### Where Witch Excels

- multi-target pressure
- elite/boss sustained DPS
- anti-healer disruption
- debuff stacking
- mid-range drain sustain
- “unavoidable” soft damage

### Where Witch Struggles

- burst race vs true DPS classes
- ranged duels at 25m+
- high-mobility assassins
- early levels without Marks
- uptime loss when kited

# 1.4 RESOURCE — HEXFLOW

Witch uses Hexflow, a two-stage ritual energy system.

### Hexflow A (Passive Flow)

- generated by applying curses
- generated by maintaining Marks
- generated by standing inside Ritual Circles
- maxes quickly but decays slowly

### Hexflow B (Ritual Charge)

- special empowerment meter
- rises from draining enemies
- rises from curse detonations
- used to amplify next ritual cast
- stored in 3-stack “Ritual Charges”

### Hexflow Uses

- amplifies DoT potency
- speeds up curse ticks
- increases Ritual Circle radius
- empowers Hexburst (burst window)
- lowers cast time on specific spells
- triggers tether duplication

### Hexflow never hard-caps

It hits diminishing returns above ~120, but continues climbing for burst prep.

# 2.0 — MECHANICAL OVERVIEW

The Witch fights by layering curses, maintaining Marks, and charging Ritual energy until a Hexburst window opens.

The four core engines:

1. Curse Engine — applies DoTs & debuffs
2. Mark Engine — marks enemies and amplifies all damage
3. Drain Engine — siphons health & Ritual Charges
4. Ritual Circle Engine — area-based pressure & Hexflow generation

All four engines feed into the Hexflow Engine, the Witch’s central scaling mechanic.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.1 — CURSE ENGINE (FOUNDATION)

Witch curses fall into 3 categories:

### A. Maledictions (Primary DoTs)

- long DoTs
- scale with Hexflow
- can crit only during detonations

### B. Afflictions (Short-DoT + Debuff)

- 6–8 second mixed effects
- weaken, slow, confuse, decay

### C. Hexbinds (Control Curses)

- root, snare, silence, slow cast
- low direct damage

### Curse Rules

- curses stack with each other
- up to 3 maledictions and 2 afflictions per target
- reapplying does NOT reset duration (it adds remaining to new duration × 0.5)
- curses are stronger inside Ritual Circles
- curses feed Hexflow passively

### Hexflow From Curses

- each active curse = +1 Hexflow/sec
- each new curse application = +3 Hexflow

Curse Engine establishes Witch’s pressure identity.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.2 — MARK ENGINE (THE REAL DPS SPIKE)

Witch marks targets with Hex Marks, her defining DPS mechanic.

### Mark Types

1. Hex Mark (Basic)
2. Binding Mark
3. Dread Mark (Elite/Boss)

### Mark Rules

- only one Mark per target
- Marks never overwrite themselves
- Marks amplify ALL damage taken by the target from Witch
- Marks stack internal “Mark Intensity” up to 100

### Mark Intensity Gain

- +2 per curse tick
- +5 per curse application
- +10 per Ritual detonation
- +15 per Drain tether full cycle

### Intensity Breakpoints

- 20 Intensity — DoT tick speed +8%
- 40 Intensity — DoT potency +15%
- 60 Intensity — Ritual Circle damage +20%
- 80 Intensity — curse detonations guaranteed crit
- 100 Intensity — next Hexburst causes AOE explosion

### Mark Duration

- base 15 sec
- extended by +0.5 sec per curse tick
- effectively permanent in sustained fights

Mark Engine creates true late-game DPS scaling, just like Priest’s Sanctity Engine.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.3 — DRAIN ENGINE (SUSTAIN + BURST PREP)

Drains convert enemy suffering into Witch empowerment.

### Drain Sources

- Life Drain
- Spirit Siphon
- Tethered Leech
- Mark siphons
- Circle drain effect (if talented)

### Drain Rules

- siphons HP and a small amount of Ritual Charge
- drains give +4 Hexflow/sec while active
- drains heal Witch for 20–30% of siphoned value
- drains feed Mark Engine Intensity
- drains are strongest at mid-range: 10–18m
- drains generate Ritual Charges for the Hexburst

### Ritual Charge Gain

- each full drain cycle: +1 Charge
- max charges: 3

### Drain Scaling

- Spell Power
- Hex Power
- Mark Intensity
- Haste (partial)

Drain Engine is Witch’s survivability + preparation loop.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.4 — RITUAL CIRCLE ENGINE (POSITIONAL PRESSURE)

Witch places circles that amplify her curses and weaken enemies.

### Circle Features

- deals AoE curse burn
- increases Hexflow regen
- speeds up DoT ticks
- slows enemies inside
- amplifies Mark Intensity gain

### Circle Radius

Base: 5m

Talented: up to 8m

### Circle Duration

Base: 8 sec

Talented: up to 12 sec

### Circle Hexflow Bonus

+10 Hexflow on placement

+3 Hexflow/sec while inside

### Enemy Debuffs Inside

- –10% accuracy
- –12% movement
- –8% magic resist (if talented)
- +20% curse tick frequency

### Boss Interaction

Bosses resist slow but take full DoT acceleration.

Ritual Circle Engine makes Witch a zone specialist like Warden but magical, not physical.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.5 — HEXFLOW ENGINE (THE CORE SCALING LOOP)

Hexflow is the Witch’s central escalation mechanic, equivalent to:

- Sanctity (Priest)
- Current (Sandstrider)
- Frost → Brittle (Frost Guard)
- Guard (Warden)

### Hexflow Gain Sources

- curses
- drain cycles
- Mark ticks
- Ritual Circles
- curse detonations
- talent passives

### Hexflow Decay

- out of combat: –5 every 3 sec
- in combat: –3 every 8 sec if no curses active
- drain active: pauses decay
- standing in circle: pauses decay

### Hexflow Breakpoints

20 Hexflow — Minor Hexforce

- DoT potency +5%

40 Hexflow — Stable Ritual

- DoT tick speed +10%

60 Hexflow — Binding Surge

- drains +15% stronger
- circles +1m radius

80 Hexflow — Major Omencraft

- curses crit on detonation
- Ritual Charge generation +1

100 Hexflow — HEXBURST READY

Next Hexburst:

- detonates all curses
- unleashes AoE burst
- massively boosts Mark Intensity
- resets Ritual Charges

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.6 — HEXBURST (THE WITCH’S BURST WINDOW)

Witch’s single burst moment, triggered at 100 Hexflow + 3 Ritual Charges.

### Hexburst Effects

- detonates all maledictions
- deals AoE curse explosion (6m)
- marks all enemies hit with Hex Mark
- applies Affliction: Ruin
- gives Witch:

– +25% DoT potency for 6s

– +20% Haste for 6s

– +1 free Ritual Circle

– +50 Hexflow (rapid re-entry)

Hexburst gives Witch her only significant burst phase and fixes her late-game boss scaling issues.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.7 — DOT INTERACTION RULES (NEW)

### DoTs DO trigger:

- Mark Intensity gains
- Hexflow gains
- Ritual Charge gains (if talented)

### DoTs DO NOT trigger:

- multi-pulse loops
- infinite-duration feedback cycles
- root/stagger interactions

### DoTs scale with:

- Spell Power (primary)
- Hex Power (secondary)
- Haste
- Mark Intensity
- Hexflow

This establishes DoTs as Witch’s real damage engine.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.8 — CONTROL & SOFT CC ENGINE

Witch is not a stun/root fiend like Warden, but uses soft CC:

- slows
- accuracy debuffs
- affliction silences
- movement decay
- stagger (rare, only from detonations)

Soft CC supports her DoT uptime without overshadowing physical controllers.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 2.9 — SPECIAL PASSIVE: HEX ECHO

“When you curse a target more than 20 meters away:

- +1 Hexflow
- +1 Ritual Charge (20 sec cooldown)”

Gives Witch a fair ranged compensation tool.

## SECTION 6 — CLASS SYSTEMS & AI INTERACTION

### Witch — Hexflow / Curse / Mark / Ritual Circle / Drain Logic

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.0 — SYSTEM OVERVIEW

Witch gameplay is governed by five interconnected engines:

1. Curse Engine — sustained DoT & debuff system
2. Mark Engine — dynamic target amplification
3. Drain Engine — sustain + Ritual Charge generator
4. Ritual Circle Engine — positional amplification zone
5. Hexflow Engine — escalation → burst cycle

These systems produce the Witch’s identity:

slow pressure → rising threat → explosion → reset → repeat.

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.1 — CURSE ENGINE LOGIC

### Curse Application Rules

- Witch may apply up to 3 Maledictions + 2 Afflictions per target
- New application extends duration (remaining × 0.5)
- All curses scale with:

– Spell Power

– Hex Power

– Mark Intensity

– Hexflow

– Haste

### Curse Tick Rules

Every tick:

- +1 Hexflow
- +2 Mark Intensity
- triggers Affliction passive effects (slow, weaken, etc.)
- accelerated by Ritual Circles
- Maledictions tick more frequently at Hexflow ≥40
- Maledictions crit on detonation at Hexflow ≥80

### Curse Spread Rules

- Maledictions may spread (if talented) at 12% per tick
- Spread carries half remaining duration
- Spread does NOT overwrite existing curses

### AI Interaction with Curses

Enemy AI:

- attempts to cleanse Afflictions first
- avoids entering Circle zones if heavily cursed
- tries to bait Witch to reapply curses inefficiently
- interrupts Witch during multi-curse windows

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.2 — MARK ENGINE (TARGET AMPLIFICATION)

### Mark Application Rules

- 1 Mark per target
- Mark duration: 15 sec (extends by curse ticks)
- Mark Intensity cap: 100
- Intensity scales with:

– curse ticks

– drain ticks

– Ritual detonations

– Witch talents

### Mark Intensity Thresholds

20 — DoT speed +8%

40 — DoT potency +15%

60 — Ritual Circle damage +20%

80 — DoT detonations crit

100 — Hexburst detonates all curses AoE

### Mark Detonation Rules

When detonated:

- deal burst equal to 2–3 ticks of each active DoT
- apply Ruin (if talented)
- create minor stagger on non-boss enemies
- heal Witch for 5% max HP (baseline)

### AI Response to Marks

- bosses gain temporary resist to Intensity spikes
- elites will disengage briefly if they reach ≥80 Intensity
- humanoids may use defensive cooldowns at ≥60 Intensity
- beasts enrage when marked above 90 Intensity

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.3 — DRAIN ENGINE LOGIC

### Drain Cycle Rules

Drain effects tick every 0.5–1.0 sec depending on Haste.

Each tick:

- heals Witch
- deals magic damage
- +4 Hexflow
- +5 Mark Intensity
- contributes to Ritual Charges

### Drain Break Rules

Drain breaks when:

- target moves beyond 20m
- Witch is CC’d
- target becomes immune
- Witch cancels channel

Upon break:

- Witch retains 50% of accumulated Ritual energy for 4 seconds
- drains apply “Residual Siphon” (DoT for 4 seconds)

### AI Response to Drains

- ranged mobs backpedal out of tether range
- melee mobs attempt to interrupt Drain
- elites use controlled bursts to break channel
- bosses maintain uptime but counter by applying anti-heal debuffs

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.4 — RITUAL CIRCLE ENGINE LOGIC

### Circle Placement Rules

- placed at Witch’s feet or target location
- 5–8m radius (based on talents)
- persists 8–12 seconds
- multiple circles allowed (up to 2 baseline with mastery)

### Circle Effects on Witch

Inside circle:

- +3 Hexflow/sec
- +20% DoT tick speed
- +6–10% DR vs magic
- drain channels +10% stronger
- Hexflow decay paused

### Circle Effects on Enemies

Inside circle:

- DoT ticks 20–28% faster
- –10% accuracy
- –12% movement
- mark intensity +20% faster
- at Hexflow ≥80: take 10% bonus magic damage

### Circle Detonation (if talented)

- circle expires → minor AoE pop
- damage scales with number of curses on enemies inside
- increases Hexflow by +12

### AI Response to Circles

- intelligent units attempt to leave circle
- beasts stay unless heavily debuffed
- elites step in only during burst windows
- bosses ignore circle but take full accelerated DoT damage

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.5 — HEXFLOW ENGINE (ESCALATION LOOP)

### Hexflow Gain Rules

+1 per curse tick

+3 per curse application

+4 per drain tick

+10 on Ritual Circle placement

+5 on Mark Detonation

+5–15 from certain talents

Hexflow max: 100 before Hexburst.

### Hexflow Decay Rules

Out of Combat: –5 per 3 sec

In Combat: –3 per 8 sec if no curses present

Drain active: decay paused

In circle: decay paused

### Hexflow Threshold Behavior

20 — Minor Hexforce

40 — Stable Ritual

60 — Binding Surge

80 — Major Omencraft

100 — Hexburst triggers

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.6 — HEXBURST SYSTEM

### Trigger Conditions

- 100 Hexflow
- 3 Ritual Charges

### Hexburst Effects

- detonates all active curses on all marked targets
- applies Ruin (heavy Affliction debuff)
- deals AoE dark-flame burst
- grants:

– +25% DoT potency for 6 sec

– +20% Haste for 6 sec

– 1 free Ritual Circle

– +50 Hexflow (re-entry spike)

- resets Ritual Charges

### AI Response

- elites back off during Hexburst animation
- ranged mobs temporarily stop attacking
- bosses reduce incoming burst by 10% (built-in mitigation)

────────────────────────────────────────

# 6.7 — CONTROL & SOFT CC ENGINE

Witch control is primarily soft CC:

### Soft CC Sources

- Afflictions
- Circle slows
- Accuracy debuffs
- Curse-based movement decay
- Hexburst stagger

### CC Rules

- slows and accuracy penalties stack (up to caps)
- Witch has no hard stuns except via talents
- boss slow = 30% effective
- elite slow = 60% effective
- humanoid enemies react by cleansing Afflictions

### Melee AI

- zigzags to break curse reapplication
- interrupts drains aggressively
- avoids standing in circles unless enraged
- flees at ≥80 Mark Intensity

### Ranged AI

- kites beyond 20m to break drains
- tries to outrange Curse Echoes
- focuses Witch during channeling
- moves out of circles quickly

### Elite AI

- intentionally triggers Hexflow decay windows
- cycles between stepping in/out of circles
- uses anti-magic bursts to punish channel casts

### Boss AI

- ignores circles but takes all benefits
- attempts to disrupt Ritual Charges
- forces movement to break curse uptime
- counters Hexburst with short DR window

- spreads curses to adds automatically

- repositions constantly to renew curses
