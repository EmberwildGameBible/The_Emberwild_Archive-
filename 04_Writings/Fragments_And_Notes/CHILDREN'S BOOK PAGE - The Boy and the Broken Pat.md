# CHILDREN’S BOOK PAGE — “The Boy and the Broken Path”

*From a tattered beginner’s reader found in a Heartland school.*

> The boy went too far from the house.
> 
> 
> He saw broken ground and kept walking.
> 
> He didn’t listen when Mother said,
> 
> “Stops mean stop.”
> 

> The ground opened under him.
> 
> 
> The boy fell.
> 
> The boy didn’t come back.
> 

> The End.
>
