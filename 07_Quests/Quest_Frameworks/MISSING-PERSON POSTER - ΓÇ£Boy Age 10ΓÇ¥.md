# MISSING-PERSON POSTER — “Boy Age 10”

*Posted on a Heartland gate.*

**Missing:**

Boy, age 10, brown hair, thin.

Name: Orven Dael.

Last seen near the creek behind the mill.

Left home without shoes.

Mother says he likes wandering.

If you see him, send word to the Dael house.

Reward offered.

No questions.
