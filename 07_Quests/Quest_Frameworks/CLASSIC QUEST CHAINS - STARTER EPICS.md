# CLASSIC QUEST CHAINS - STARTER EPICS

Low-to-mid level questlines built to echo classic MMO pacing: breadcrumb starts, dungeon touchpoints, class beats, and memorable blue-quality rewards.

## QUICK LIST
- **The Broken Watchpost (Lv 12–16):** Shoreline defense arc with a mini-dungeon hook.
- **Ashen Ember Trials (Lv 18–22):** Class-agnostic rite with role-specific challenges.
- **The Lost Artisan (Lv 20–24):** Profession questline that teaches rare recipes.
- **Beast of the Bramble Vale (Lv 24–26):** Elite outdoor hunt for small groups.
- **Stormbound Courier (Lv 26–28):** Timed relay quest that pushes travel planning.

---

## THE BROKEN WATCHPOST (Lv 12–16)
**Hook:** Pick up a splintered signal horn from a fallen guard near the coast.

**Beats:**
1. **Recon the Palisade:** Scout three ruined watchtowers and mark enemy banners.
2. **Shoreline Skirmish:** Kill-count quest vs. raiders, collect horn reeds for repairs.
3. **Hold the Gate:** 5-player micro-event; defend the palisade for 3 waves.
4. **Dungeon Tie-In:** Breadcrumb to a nearby low-level dungeon; optional for bonus blue shield.

**Rewards:** Blue tank shield or ranged quiver, plus +100 reputation with the coastal faction.

**Notes:** Encourages early grouping without locking story behind instance.

---

## ASHEN EMBER TRIALS (Lv 18–22)
**Hook:** Veteran NPC in a capital city offers a rite of passage open to all classes.

**Beats:**
1. **Role Trials:**
   - Tank: Hold aggro on three elite sparks for 60 seconds.
   - Healer: Sustain three injured NPCs without letting health drop below 40%.
   - DPS: Break three flame totems within a timed window.
2. **Resource Mastery:** Collect fuel nodes (Rage Briquettes, Mana Crystals, Wind Sigils) that match your class resource.
3. **Culmination Duel:** Solo instance against a mirror image that uses your class toolkit.

**Rewards:** Blue-quality class trinket (threat for tanks, regen for healers, crit/haste for DPS) and a cosmetic “Emberbrand” tabard.

**Notes:** Reusable yearly; good candidate for future holiday events.

## THE LOST ARTISAN (Lv 20–24)
**Hook:** Rumors of a reclusive cook/armorer hidden in a backwater inn.

**Beats:**
1. **Gather Proof:** Bring rare ingredients or ore samples (Sunpepper, Frostthistle, Hearthgrain Malt, Azurefin Fillet).
2. **Prototype Craft:** Perform a timed craft at an anvil or stove while fending off saboteurs.
3. **Secure the Workshop:** Escort quest to move the artisan’s tools to a safer hub.

**Rewards:**
- **Cooking:** Unlocks *Stormcall Soup* and *Vanguard Roast* recipes.
- **Blacksmithing:** Unlocks a +Fire Resist helm and a +Defense shield pattern.
- **Optional:** Profession XP boost and a hearthstone cooldown reducer toy.

**Notes:** Gives lowbie professions meaningful power spikes without auction reliance.

---

## BEAST OF THE BRAMBLE VALE (Lv 24–26)
**Hook:** Wanted poster in town square calls for a group to deal with a regenerating thornbeast.

**Beats:**
1. **Track the Trail:** Follow claw marks and sap pools; avoid root snares.
2. **Sap Sample:** Collect resin from thornlings to craft a slowing salve.
3. **Group Hunt:** 3–5 player elite fight; beast enrages at 30% and summons briar adds.
4. **Trophy Claim:** Harvest hide/antlers; deliver to taxidermist or leatherworker.

**Rewards:** Blue-quality leather chest (Agility/Stamina) or two-handed staff (Intellect/Spirit), plus cosmetic wall trophy.

**Notes:** Outdoor elite content to encourage grouping before dungeons dominate leveling.

---

## STORMBOUND COURIER (Lv 26–28)
**Hook:** Intercept a battered courier satchel on a storm road.

**Beats:**
1. **Time Window:** Deliver three sealed packets to watchtowers within 10 minutes (mount optional but helpful).
2. **Weather Hazards:** Lightning strikes and wind gusts can dismount; players must choose safe routes.
3. **Counter-Intercept:** Ambush by rival couriers; optional duel for extra reputation.

**Rewards:** Cloak with movement speed and nature resist, plus a reusable courier horn (minor sprint on 15m cooldown).

**Notes:** Teaches travel planning and route knowledge; ideal for zones with variable weather from the travel chapters.
