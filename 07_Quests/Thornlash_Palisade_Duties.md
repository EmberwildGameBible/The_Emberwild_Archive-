# Thornlash Palisade Duties

**Origin/Provenance:** Wall watch rotation started as a ferry captain’s honor guard; the Slidecaller Guild repurposed it to keep the ash drifts clear for sleds and smugglers.

**Cultural Tie:** Marrowhook salt crews and Slidecaller lookouts trade ledger marks; whichever faction fills the [Sentry Ration Docket](../09_Artifacts/Sentry_Ration_Docket.md) first claims the best bunk space.

**Gameplay Hook:** Repeatable trio of quests for levels 22–26 that rotate between wall repair, ash drift scouting, and ration inspections. Completing all three in a day grants a discount on [Thornlash Sentry Wristwraps](../08_Items/Gear/Thornlash_Sentry_Wristwraps.md) and reputation with both factions.

## Duty Rotation
1. **Pitch the Planks** — Apply resin to cracked beams using rope grease from [Frostbriar Reach](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Zone_Index.md). See also: [Thornlash_Palisade](../02_Regions/Thornlash_Palisade.md), [Thornlash_Warning_Slate](../09_Artifacts/Thornlash_Warning_Slate.md), [Windbreak_Bounty_Board](Windbreak_Bounty_Board.md). Spawns a fresh entry on the warning slate.
2. **Ash Drift Sweep** — Track ash vortices and cull [Thornlash Prowler](../03_Lore/Creatures/Thornlash_Prowler.md) packs stirred up by the gusts. See also: [Old_Road_Circuit](Old_Road_Circuit.md), [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md), [Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub](Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md). Updates the ration docket when pelts are turned in.
3. **Ration Audit** — Inspect crates logged on the [Sentry Ration Docket](../09_Artifacts/Sentry_Ration_Docket.md) and seize salt bricks stamped with contraband brands. See also: [Strandline_Courier_Loop](Strandline_Courier_Loop.md), [Saltmarrow_Strand](../02_Regions/Saltmarrow_Strand/Saltmarrow_Strand.md), [Marrowhook_Ledger_Dockets](../09_Artifacts/Marrowhook_Ledger_Dockets.md). Flags a follow-up lead on smugglers in the [Windbreak_Bounty_Board](Windbreak_Bounty_Board.md).
