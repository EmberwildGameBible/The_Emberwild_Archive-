# Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub

**Origin/Provenance:** Ferry contracts posted at Lantern Barge Mooring after increased Gleam Surges.

**Cultural Tie:** Pole-walkers tally rope grease debts in knots; Saltmarrow traders insist on stamped glowspore seals.

**Gameplay Hook:** Hub of 7 quests (2 repeatables) leading players through ropeway and glowspore loops toward Ashen Cry rumors.

## Quests
1. **Pole Grease Run** — Deliver rope grease to Peatcoil Waypoint. Repeatable. See also: [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md), [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md), [Peatcoil_Root_Band](../08_Items/Gear/Peatcoil_Root_Band.md). Spawns [Pole_Grease_Chit](../09_Artifacts/Pole_Grease_Chit.md).
2. **Glowspore Escort** — Protect carriers during a Gleam Surge Response. See also: [Glowspore_Tallow](../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md), [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs.md). Spawns [Glowspore_Carrier_Route](../09_Artifacts/Glowspore_Carrier_Route.md).
3. **Ventwatch Bells** — Ring vent bells to warn of cult divers. See also: [Ventwatch_Bounty_Note](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bounty_Note.md), [Ashen_Vent_Key](../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md), [Ashen_Cry_Grotto](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md). Spawns [Ventwatch_Bell_Log](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bell_Log.md).
4. **Ledger Salvage** — Recover ledgers from Sunken Ledger Wreck. Repeatable. See also: [Sodden_Ledger_Chain](../09_Artifacts/Sodden_Ledger_Chain.md), [Glowspore_Trade_Scrip](../08_Items/Trinkets/Glowspore_Trade_Scrip.md), [Lantern_Barge_Warning](../09_Artifacts/Lantern_Barge_Warning.md). Spawns [Ledger_Salvage_Record](../09_Artifacts/Ledger_Salvage_Record.md).
5. **Gleam Eel Baiting** — Catch Gleam Eels for cooking orders. See also: [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md), [Frostbite_Pike_Pole](../08_Items/Fishing/Frostbite_Pike_Pole.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Zone_Overview.md).
