# Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub

**Origin/Provenance:** Ferry contracts posted at Lantern Barge Mooring after increased Gleam Surges and new Frostbriar convoy ties.

**Cultural Tie:** Pole-walkers tally rope grease debts in knots; Saltmarrow traders insist on stamped glowspore seals traded alongside [Glimmer Brew](../08_Items/items_table.csv) jars.

**Gameplay Hook:** Hub of 12 quests (4 repeatables, 2 mini chains, 1 capstone) leading players through ropeway, glowspore, and envoy loops toward [Ashen Cry Grotto](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md) rumors and [Frostbriar_Trail_Hub](Frostbriar_Trail_Hub.md) caravans while rewarding specific pulls from [items_table.csv](../08_Items/items_table.csv).

## Quests
1. **Pole Grease Run** — Deliver rope grease and [Greatwood Arrow](../08_Items/items_table.csv) lashings to Peatcoil Waypoint. Repeatable. See also: [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md), [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md). Spawns [Pole_Grease_Knotwork](../09_Artifacts/Pole_Grease_Knotwork.md).
2. **Ledger Salvage** — Recover ledgers from Sunken Ledger Wreck; trade recovered [Jade Urn](../08_Items/items_table.csv) seals for bonus pay. Repeatable. See also: [Glowspore_Trade_Scrip](../08_Items/Trinkets/Glowspore_Trade_Scrip.md), [Lantern_Barge_Warning](../09_Artifacts/Lantern_Barge_Warning.md). Spawns [Ledger_Dragline_Map](../09_Artifacts/Ledger_Dragline_Map.md).
3. **Ropeway Repairs** — Replace cracked planks using ash vent bolts and [Ironroot Oil](../08_Items/items_table.csv) tar. Repeatable. See also: [Mire_Salvage_Chain](Mire_Salvage_Chain.md), [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md). Spawns [Ropeway_Repair_Docket](../09_Artifacts/Ropeway_Repair_Docket.md).
4. **Gleam Eel Baiting** — Catch Gleam Eels for cooking orders and barter [Lantern Harpoon](../08_Items/items_table.csv) barbs for extras. Repeatable. See also: [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md), [Frostbite_Pike_Pole](../08_Items/Fishing/Frostbite_Pike_Pole.md). Spawns [Gleam_Eel_Cleaning_List](../09_Artifacts/Gleam_Eel_Cleaning_List.md).
5. **Glowspore Escort** — Protect carriers during a Gleam Surge Response; stage [Lantern Cowl](../08_Items/items_table.csv) wards at each bell. See also: [Glowspore_Tallow](../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs.md). Spawns [Glowspore_Carrier_Route](../09_Artifacts/Glowspore_Carrier_Route.md).
6. **Ventwatch Bells** — Ring vent bells to warn of cult divers and deliver [Luminous Elixir](../08_Items/items_table.csv) to bellkeepers. See also: [Ventwatch_Bounty_Note](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bounty_Note.md), [Ashen_Vent_Key](../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md). Spawns [Ventwatch_Bell_Log](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bell_Log.md).
7. **Lantern Barge Refit** — Patch hull scorch with [Hazel Driftleaf](../08_Items/items_table.csv) tar mats and install [Lantern Needle](../08_Items/items_table.csv) ground wires; ties into [NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES](NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md) ferry stocks. See also: [Lantern_Barge_Warning](../09_Artifacts/Lantern_Barge_Warning.md), [Frostbriar_Trail_Hub](Frostbriar_Trail_Hub.md). Spawns [Ventwatch_Anchor_Slate](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Anchor_Slate.md).
8. **Mini Chain — Counterbalance Checks (Step 1):** Inspect Peatcoil braces with [Kindled Yarn](../08_Items/items_table.csv) tension gauges to stabilize ropeways. See also: [Pole_Grease_Chit](../09_Artifacts/Pole_Grease_Chit.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md). Spawns [Glowspore_Tithe_Slip](../09_Artifacts/Glowspore_Tithe_Slip.md).
9. **Mini Chain — Counterbalance Checks (Step 2):** Test weighted glowspore crates using [Luminous Parchment](../08_Items/items_table.csv) checklists and report to [Sporescout Quartermaster Oru](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#sporescout-quartermaster-oru-—-mycelial-staging-post). See also: [Glimmerdeep_Mire](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Zone_Overview.md), [Hollowroot_Warren](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Hollowroot_Warren/Dungeon_Overview.md). Spawns [Glowspore_Tally_Tab](../09_Artifacts/Glowspore_Tally_Tab.md).
10. **Mini Chain — Salt Truce Envoys (Step 1):** Escort Saltmarrow traders carrying [Barley Snack Pack](../08_Items/items_table.csv) and [Cinderbrew Flask](../08_Items/items_table.csv) samples to ropeway councils. See also: [Strandline_Courier_Loop](Strandline_Courier_Loop.md), [Frostbriar_Trail_Hub](Frostbriar_Trail_Hub.md). Spawns [Ledger_Salvage_Record](../09_Artifacts/Ledger_Salvage_Record.md).
11. **Mini Chain — Salt Truce Envoys (Step 2):** Negotiate shared tolls with Frostbriar sledders; award [Deepwatch Pauldrons](../08_Items/items_table.csv) commended by [Glacier Lodge Quartermaster Bryn](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#glacier-lodge-quartermaster-bryn-—-frostmarch-lodge). See also: [Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements.md), [Icefloe_Toll_Ledger](../09_Artifacts/Icefloe_Toll_Ledger.md). Spawns [Shoreline_Peace_Accord](../09_Artifacts/Shoreline_Peace_Accord.md).
12. **Capstone — Lantern Barge Accord:** Finalize shared defense between ropeway bells and Frostbriar relays; delivers [Dawnstar Talisman](../08_Items/items_table.csv) blessings before opening a barge path to the [Ashen Cry Grotto entrance](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md). See also: [NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES](NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md), [Old_Road_Circuit](Old_Road_Circuit.md). Spawns [Shoreline_Peace_Accord](../09_Artifacts/Shoreline_Peace_Accord.md).
