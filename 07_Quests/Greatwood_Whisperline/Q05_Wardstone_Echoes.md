## Implementation Notes
- Chalk interaction at wardstone consumes packets; each groove requires one use.
- Horn use spawns visual hum rings; requires standing on marked cairn points.
- Ribbon replacement uses item pickup at milepost sapling; respawns after completion.
- Wisps spawn when horn is blown; moderate threat for level band.
- Failure if player leaves area during horn sequence; progress resets to last completed tone.
