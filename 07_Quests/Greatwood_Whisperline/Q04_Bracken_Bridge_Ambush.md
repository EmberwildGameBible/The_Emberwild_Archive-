## Implementation Notes
- Place snare nodes near bridge ramps; use hatchet to disable.
- Smuggler's Knot Hooks consumed on planting; place at flagged vines around Bracken Snare Ring.
- Ward-Chalk Packets taken from patrol satchels; stealth reduces aggro radius.
- Quest fails if player kills more than five wardens; reset counter on abandon.
- Shared progress for group planting, individual credit for chalk theft.
