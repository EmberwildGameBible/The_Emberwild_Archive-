# Mire_Salvage_Chain

**Origin/Provenance:** Pole-walker dispute over missing ledgers escalated into a salvage feud.

**Cultural Tie:** Ropeway crews mark each stage with knotted cords; Marrowhook traders demand signed glowspore seals for every recovered box.

**Gameplay Hook:** 5-quest chain tracing lost ledgers from Glimmerdeep to Ashen Cry entrances; seeds future dungeon reputation line.

## Chain Steps
1. **Missing Box Report** — Interview ferrymen about lost scrip boxes. See also: [Glowspore_Trade_Scrip](../08_Items/Trinkets/Glowspore_Trade_Scrip.md), [Lantern_Barge_Warning](../09_Artifacts/Lantern_Barge_Warning.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_Settlements](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_Settlements.md). Spawns [Missing_Box_Notice](../09_Artifacts/Missing_Box_Notice.md).
2. **Sodden Ledger Hunt** — Dive the Sunken Ledger Wreck for chained pages. See also: [Sodden_Ledger_Chain](../09_Artifacts/Sodden_Ledger_Chain.md), [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_Subareas_and_POIs.md). Spawns [Ledger_Diver_Brief](../09_Artifacts/Ledger_Diver_Brief.md).
3. **Vent Ash Tracking** — Follow ash sigils to locate stolen boxes. See also: [Ashen_Vent_Key](../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md), [Ashen_Cry_Grotto](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md), [Ventwatch_Bounty_Note](../09_Artifacts/Ventwatch_Bounty_Note.md). Spawns [Ash_Track_Map](../09_Artifacts/Ash_Track_Map.md).
4. **Cult Ledger Broker** — Negotiate with an Ash Drift envoy for the final chains. See also: [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md), [Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs.md), [Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub](Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md). Spawns [Ledger_Broker_Receipt](../09_Artifacts/Ledger_Broker_Receipt.md).
5. **Seal the Smug Route** — Close the ropeway cache feeding Ashen Cry smugglers. See also: [Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests](Dungeon/Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests.md), [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md).
