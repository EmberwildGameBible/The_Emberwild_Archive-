# Old_Road_Circuit

**Origin/Provenance:** Cinderfen caravan captain notes repurposed by Windbreak couriers to connect Frostbriar and Saltmarrow.

**Cultural Tie:** Drivers carve old-road hashmarks into sled rails; Marrowhook traders pay in dried kelp rations for safe passage.

**Gameplay Hook:** Repeatable patrol loop (3 legs) linking Frostbriar Reach and Saltmarrow Strand; awards courier tokens and sled upgrade parts.

## Circuit Legs
1. **Shiverhook to Strandline** — Carry repair kits to Saltmarrow palisade. 2. **Strandline to Meltwater** — Escort fish salt to Frostbriar camps.
