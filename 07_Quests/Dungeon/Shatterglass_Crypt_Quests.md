# Shatterglass_Crypt_Quests

**Origin/Provenance:** Oathboard postings outside Riverwatch Cathedral for challengers entering the Crypt.

**Cultural Tie:** Written in clipped Riverwatch script with Glimmerdeep trade stamps to prove the posters survived the entry gauntlet.

**Gameplay Hook:** Four hooks align Shardbound Covenant set acquisition with reflect and phase mechanics.

1. **Hold the Heat** — Keep [Refraction_Dust_Vial](../../08_Items/Crafting/Refraction_Dust_Vial.md) buffs active while kiting Cinder Wyrmling to secure [Shardbound_Oath_Helmet](../../08_Items/Gear/Shardbound_Oath_Helmet.md) and [Magma_Spines_Pendant](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Magma_Spines_Pendant.md). Spawns [Shardbound_Challenge_Note](../../09_Artifacts/Shardbound_Challenge_Note.md).
2. **Silence the Mirror** — Interrupt cone cleaves on Mirror-Lich and read [Lich_Prism_Diary](../../08_Items/Documents/Lich_Prism_Diary.md) at a prism node to lock in [Shardbound_Oath_Breastplate](../../08_Items/Gear/Shardbound_Oath_Breastplate.md).
3. **Chain the Prophet** — Interrupt both phase swaps then finish the fight while holding [Shatterglass_Crypt_Keyshard](../../08_Items/Keys/Shatterglass_Crypt_Keyshard.md) to guarantee [Shardbound_Route_Boots](../../08_Items/Gear/Shardbound_Route_Boots.md) and [Prophet_Ashen_Visor](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Prophet_Ashen_Visor.md).
4. **Weave the Oath** — Deliver [Prismthread_Reinforcement](../../08_Items/Crafting/Prismthread_Reinforcement.md) to the Crypt anvil to unlock bonus rolls on [Mirror_Lich_Shardsleeve](../../08_Items/Gear/Mirror_Lich_Shardsleeve.md) and [Prophet_Voicecage_Spear](../../08_Items/Gear/Prophet_Voicecage_Spear.md).
