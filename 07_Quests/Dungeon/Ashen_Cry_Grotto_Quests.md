# Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests

**Origin/Provenance:** Shiverhook Outwatch contracts issued after the first ash vent collapse.

**Cultural Tie:** Windbreak sled crews sign the contracts with briar notches; Glimmerdeep pole-walkers demand glowspore hazard pay.

**Gameplay Hook:** Mix of 8 quests guiding players through the dungeon, each spawning an artifact and referencing local items and creatures.

1. **Seal the Slide Breach** — Collect [Slidecaller_Key_Fragment](../../08_Items/Keys/Slidecaller_Key_Fragment.md) from Slidebreaker Tandem to lock a collapsing bridge. See also: [Ashen_Cry_Grotto](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md), [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md), [White_Howl_Rumor_Slip](../../09_Artifacts/White_Howl_Rumor_Slip.md). Spawns [Slidecaller_Breach_Report](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Breach_Report.md).
2. **Douse the Embermouth** — Use meltwater buckets to extinguish Embermouth Vicar’s vents. See also: [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md), [Frostbriar_Reach](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Zone_Overview.md), [Meltwater_Bracer](../../08_Items/Gear/Meltwater_Bracer.md). Spawns [Embermouth_Dousing_Order](../../09_Artifacts/Embermouth_Dousing_Order.md).
3. **Lantern Line Recovery** — Retrieve rope grips from Cinderline Skitter for pole-walkers. See also: [Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves](../../08_Items/Gear/Lantern-Line_Grip_Gloves.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire](../../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Zone_Overview.md), [Glowspore_Tallow](../../08_Items/Crafting/Glowspore_Tallow.md). Spawns [Rope_Grip_Shipping_Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Rope_Grip_Shipping_Slip.md).
4. **Gleamdrain Cleanup** — Burn out leech sacs from Gleamdrain Leechking. See also: [Glowspore_Tallow_Lure](../../08_Items/Fishing/Glowspore_Tallow_Lure.md), [Gleam_Eel_Filets](../../08_Items/Cooking/Gleam_Eel_Filets.md), [Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event](../../02_Regions/Glimmerdeep_Mire/Glimmerdeep_Mire_World_Event.md). Spawns [Leechking_Bounty_Post](../../09_Artifacts/Leechking_Bounty_Post.md).
5. **Ashen Key Custody** — Secure [Ashen_Vent_Key](../../08_Items/Keys/Ashen_Vent_Key.md) from the Hierarch. See also: [Ashen_Cry_Lantern_Pike](../../08_Items/Fishing/Ashen_Cry_Lantern_Pike.md), [Glowspore_Trade_Scrip](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Glowspore_Trade_Scrip.md), [Mire_Salvage_Chain](../../07_Quests/Mire_Salvage_Chain.md). Spawns [Hierarch_Custody_Letter](../../09_Artifacts/Hierarch_Custody_Letter.md).
6. **Bridge Bolt Audit** — Collect sled bolts from Slidebreaker Tandem debris. See also: [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md), [Old_Road_Circuit](../../07_Quests/Old_Road_Circuit.md), [Frostbite_Warden](../../03_Lore/Creatures/Frostbite_Warden.md). Spawns [Bolt_Audit_Ledger](../../09_Artifacts/Bolt_Audit_Ledger.md).
7. **Ashfall Samples** — Gather ash cores for Windbreak herbalists. See also: [Gnat-Smother_Salve](../../08_Items/Crafting/Gnat-Smother_Salve.md), [Ashbound_Breath_Charm](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashbound_Breath_Charm.md), [Ash_Drift_Hollows](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs.md). Spawns [Ashfall_Sample_Satchel](../../09_Artifacts/Ashfall_Sample_Satchel.md).
8. **Bells of Warning** — Ring Slidecaller bells inside the grotto to warn Timberline Post. See also: [Slidecaller_Bell_Tab](../../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Bell_Tab.md), [Frostbriar_Reach_World_Event](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_World_Event.md), [Shiverhook_Outwatch](../../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements.md). Spawns [Grotto_Bell_Report](../../09_Artifacts/Grotto_Bell_Report.md).
