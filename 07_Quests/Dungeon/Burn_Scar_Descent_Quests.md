# Burn_Scar_Descent_Quests

**Origin/Provenance:** Freight Foreman Letha’s contract board after the first slag convoys survived the Descent.

**Cultural Tie:** Emberford runners stamp each contract with soot fingerprints; Riverwatch scouts add prism etchings for mirror lanes.

**Gameplay Hook:** Four quest hooks tie Slagrunner Signal set pieces and signature drops to vent safety milestones.

1. **Mark the Flares** — Use [Gatewarden_Fissure_Chart](../../08_Items/Documents/Gatewarden_Fissure_Chart.md) to tag three fissure lanes. Set reward prefers [Slagrunner_Blast_Mask](../../08_Items/Gear/Slagrunner_Blast_Mask.md). Spawns [Slagrunner_Log](../../09_Artifacts/Slagrunner_Log.md).
2. **Bolt the Freight** — Secure latches with [Burn_Scar_Ember_Latch_Key](../../08_Items/Keys/Burn_Scar_Ember_Latch_Key.md) before Root-King enrages. Rewards [Slagrunner_Heatbrace](../../08_Items/Gear/Slagrunner_Heatbrace.md) chances and references [Burn_Scar_Freight_Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Burn_Scar_Freight_Permit.md).
3. **Mirror the Judge** — Reflect at least two vent flares to earn [Prism_Verdict_Signet](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Prism_Verdict_Signet.md) and the final [Slagrunner_Treadguards](../../08_Items/Gear/Slagrunner_Treadguards.md) roll. Spawns a ledger note inside [Slagrunner_Log](../../09_Artifacts/Slagrunner_Log.md).
4. **Quench the Crew** — Deliver six [Ventflare_Resin_Tonic](../../08_Items/Crafting/Ventflare_Resin_Tonic.md) to freight crews mid-run. Grants bonus rolls on [Gatewarden_Ventbreaker_Bulwark](../../08_Items/Gear/Gatewarden_Ventbreaker_Bulwark.md) and [Root_King_Bridgebound_Talisman](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Root_King_Bridgebound_Talisman.md).
