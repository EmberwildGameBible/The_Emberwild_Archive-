# Hollowroot_Warren_Quests

**Origin/Provenance:** Contract leaflets pinned to Riverwatch’s sporeproof noticeboard after clans begged for tunnel relief.

**Cultural Tie:** Notes are written in Riverwatch shorthand with Hollowroot spiral signatures and Glimmerdeep ink smears.

**Gameplay Hook:** Four hooks guide players through Rootbound Mycelium set collection and boss interrupts.

1. **Mirror the Sentinel** — Capture markings with [Glass_Sentinel_Mirrorguard](../../08_Items/Gear/Glass_Sentinel_Mirrorguard.md) and avoid duplicate marks to unlock [Rootbound_Sporeveil](../../08_Items/Gear/Rootbound_Sporeveil.md). Spawns [Rootbound_Path_Brief](../../09_Artifacts/Rootbound_Path_Brief.md).
2. **Silence the Vent** — Interrupt Vent-Siren’s shrieks while carrying [Sporeshroud_Tonic](../../08_Items/Crafting/Sporeshroud_Tonic.md). Rewards [Rootbound_Trunkplate](../../08_Items/Gear/Rootbound_Trunkplate.md) chance and [Siren_Throatspore_Flute](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Siren_Throatspore_Flute.md).
3. **Temper the Forgemaw** — Let adds live until the final ignite, then claim [Forgemaw_Corebrand](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Forgemaw_Corebrand.md) and [Forgemaw_Emberbite_Cudgel](../../08_Items/Gear/Forgemaw_Emberbite_Cudgel.md). Finalizes [Rootbound_Warden_Greaves](../../08_Items/Gear/Rootbound_Warden_Greaves.md) drop protection.
4. **Thread the Warren** — Deliver bundles of [Mycelial_Threadspool](../../08_Items/Crafting/Mycelial_Threadspool.md) to side rooms opened with [Hollowroot_Warren_Keyroot](../../08_Items/Keys/Hollowroot_Warren_Keyroot.md) for bonus set rolls.
