# 2025-12-24 Legendary Chains — Pack Emberwild

Twelve short three-step chains, each tied to a Legendary drop (IDs 50500–50511) and their rumor/ledger artifacts.

## SCORIA DRAW (Emberford Scoria-Draw Harpoon — 50500)
- **Quest 1: Ventline Briefing** — Meet Winch-Chief Nalia in [Emberford Marketwalk](../../02_Regions/Emberford_Marketwalk/Zone_Index.md); present the [Scoria Fireglass Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Emberford_Scoria_Fireglass_Permit.md).
- **Quest 2: Feed the Forges** — Collect scoria slag from vent runoff and deliver it to Windbreak Post crews; rumor proof from the [Scoria Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Emberford_Scoria_Rumor_Slip.md).
- **Quest 3: Harpoon the Redflare** — Use the [Scoria-Draw Harpoon](../../08_Items/Gear/Emberford_Scoria-Draw_Harpoon.md) to interrupt the world boss listed in [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md), then return to Nalia.

## TIDEWRIT DEBTS (Saltmarrow Tidewrit Seal — 50501)
- **Quest 1: Ledger Audit** — Collect three unpaid tabs using the [Tide Ledger Tab](../../09_Artifacts/Saltmarrow_Tide_Ledger_Tab.md) along the [Strandline Courier Loop](../Strandline_Courier_Loop.md).
- **Quest 2: Ferry Duel Marker** — Win a ferry skirmish while flying the tidecourt pennant; gather gossip from the [Tide Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Saltmarrow_Tide_Rumor_Slip.md).
- **Quest 3: Claim the Seal** — Defeat the shoal-storm herald world boss in [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) to loot the [Tidewrit Seal](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Saltmarrow_Tidewrit_Seal.md).

## RIMEBOUND SIGNAL (Frostbriar Rimebound Relay — 50502)
- **Quest 1: Switchback Survey** — Deliver emergency flares to Gravemark anchors using the [Relay Sled Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Frostbriar_Relay_Sled_Permit.md).
- **Quest 2: Whistle Codes** — Record three Slidecaller whistle patterns after reading the [Relay Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Frostbriar_Relay_Rumor_Slip.md).
- **Quest 3: Blizzard Callout** — Ring the Gravemark horn to draw the ridge warden world boss from [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) and earn the [Rimebound Relay](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Frostbriar_Rimebound_Relay.md).

## CHASM SINGER (Glimmerdeep Chasm-Singer Chimes — 50503)
- **Quest 1: Ropeway Check** — Inspect three pylons on the [Glimmerdeep Shore Hub](../Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md) route; stamp the [Chasm Salvage Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Glimmerdeep_Chasm_Salvage_Permit.md).
- **Quest 2: Echo Map** — Tune cavern echoes using hints from the [Chasm Rumor Scrap](../../09_Artifacts/Glimmerdeep_Chasm_Rumor_Scrap.md); avoid vent eruptions.
- **Quest 3: Silence the Ventboss** — Ambush the ventboss world boss per [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) and loot the [Chasm-Singer Chimes](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Glimmerdeep_Chasm-Singer_Chimes.md).

## CROWN OF RIVERS (Greatwood Grovekeeper's Crown — 50504)
- **Quest 1: Grove Blessing** — Present the [Greatwood Grove Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Greatwood_Grove_Permit.md) at the heart lodge and gather sap offerings.
- **Quest 2: Riverwatch Muster** — Escort two ferry crews along the river while citing the [Crown Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Greatwood_Crown_Rumor_Slip.md).
- **Quest 3: Face the Stag** — Defeat the stag-spirit world boss from [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) to claim the [Grovekeeper's Crown](../../08_Items/Gear/Greatwood_Grovekeepers_Crown.md).

## MATRIARCH’S OATH (Hollowroot Mycelial Matriarch's Mantle — 50505)
- **Quest 1: Midwife’s Request** — Carry the [Birthing Ledger](../../09_Artifacts/Hollowroot_Birthing_Ledger.md) to three clan hearths inside [Hollowroot Warren](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Hollowroot_Warren/Dungeon_Overview.md).
- **Quest 2: Glowcap Offering** — Use the [Matriarch Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Hollowroot_Matriarch_Rumor_Slip.md) to calm sporelings during the hard-mode setup.
- **Quest 3: Soothe the Matriarch** — Apply glowcaps then defeat the boss under the condition listed in [Hollowroot Warren Loot Tables](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Hollowroot_Warren/Loot_Tables.md) to earn the [Mycelial Matriarch's Mantle](../../08_Items/Gear/Hollowroot_Mycelial_Mantle.md).

## BASTION BANNER (Thornlash Bastion Banner — 50506)
- **Quest 1: Wall Inspection** — Present the [Banner Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Thornlash_Banner_Permit.md) to Captain Ehra and mark three breach points.
- **Quest 2: Supply the Wall** — Deliver pitch bundles while brandishing the [Banner Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Thornlash_Banner_Rumor_Slip.md) to rally guards.
- **Quest 3: Hold the Dual Front** — Repel the paired assault described in [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) and plant the [Bastion Banner](../../08_Items/Gear/Thornlash_Bastion_Banner.md).

## EMBERFALL RITE (Ashen Cry Emberfall Circlet — 50507)
- **Quest 1: Vent Checks** — Log three vent pressures in [Ashen Cry Grotto](../Dungeon/Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests.md) while holding the [Emberfall Ledger](../../09_Artifacts/Ashen_Cry_Emberfall_Ledger.md).
- **Quest 2: Hierarch’s Warning** — Read the [Emberfall Rumor](../../09_Artifacts/Ashen_Cry_Emberfall_Rumor.md) and place ashglass wards.
- **Quest 3: Extinguish and Claim** — Extinguish three vents mid-fight as per [Ashen Cry Loot Tables](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Loot_Tables.md) to secure the [Emberfall Circlet](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Ashen_Cry_Emberfall_Circlet.md).

## OATHMIRROR (Shatterglass Oathmirror — 50508)
- **Quest 1: Oathkeeper’s Seal** — Deliver mirror wax to the Crypt Judge using the [Oathkeeper Ledger](../../09_Artifacts/Shatterglass_Oathkeeper_Ledger.md).
- **Quest 2: Silence the Curse** — Study the [Oathmirror Rumor](../../09_Artifacts/Shatterglass_Oathmirror_Rumor.md) and interrupt three curse casts.
- **Quest 3: Claim the Reflection** — Stop the Crypt Judge mid-curse as detailed in [Shatterglass Crypt Loot Tables](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Shatterglass_Crypt/Loot_Tables.md) to loot the [Shatterglass Oathmirror](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Shatterglass_Oathmirror.md).

## CLINKERBREAKER (Burn Scar Clinkerbreaker Cudgel — 50509)
- **Quest 1: Freight Weigh-In** — Validate slag hauls with the [Burn Scar Freight Permit](../../09_Artifacts/Burn_Scar_Freight_Permit.md) at the descent lifts.
- **Quest 2: Stun Practice** — Follow the [Clinker Rumor](../../09_Artifacts/Burn_Scar_Clinker_Rumor.md) to practice interrupt timings on vent golems.
- **Quest 3: Break the Colossus** — Stun the Clinker Colossus mid-swing as required in [Burn Scar Descent Loot Tables](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/Burn_Scar_Descent/Loot_Tables.md) to obtain the [Clinkerbreaker Cudgel](../../08_Items/Gear/Burn_Scar_Clinkerbreaker_Cudgel.md).

## NIGHTWARDEN (Silent Bell Nightwarden Codex — 50510)
- **Quest 1: Shadow Brief** — Present the [Nightwarden Forged Pass](../../09_Artifacts/Nightwarden_Forged_Pass.md) to the shrinekeeper in [Windbreak Bounty Board](../Windbreak_Bounty_Board.md).
- **Quest 2: Silence Coins** — Trade intel using the [Nightwarden Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Nightwarden_Rumor_Slip.md) to mark smuggler caches.
- **Quest 3: Fell the Enforcer** — Defeat the Silent Bell enforcer under the moonless condition from [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) and recover the [Nightwarden Codex](../../08_Items/Trinkets/Silent_Bell_Nightwarden_Codex.md).

## SKYLINE RUN (Windbreak Skyline Javelin — 50511)
- **Quest 1: Cable Service** — Grease ropeway anchors while logging sign-offs on the [Skyline Patrol Ledger](../../09_Artifacts/Windbreak_Skyline_Patrol_Ledger.md).
- **Quest 2: Beacon Codes** — Decode storm beacons using the [Skyline Rumor Slip](../../09_Artifacts/Windbreak_Skyline_Rumor_Slip.md) as a cipher.
- **Quest 3: Ground the Manta** — Use anchors to ground the sky-manta world boss noted in [World Boss & Elite Drop Notes](../../06_Systems/Dungeons/World_Boss_Loot.md) and claim the [Skyline Javelin](../../08_Items/Gear/Windbreak_Skyline_Javelin.md).
