# Quest Expansion Pack

Contracts, hunts, and delivery loops tying new subzones to crafting loops and bestiary research.

## [QST-001] Contract: Vent Shock
Zone: Cinderfen Hot Springs Terraces

Objective:
- Defeat 2x BST-001 Ash Myriad Vent-Tenders while their braziers are active.
- Harvest 4 Ember Sap Bark nodes without triggering vent burns.
Hooks: Forces vent-gasket crafting use and teaches players to shatter braziers before heavy pulls.
Rewards: 65 silver, +150 regional rep, and choice of Heat-Tempered Plate shard bundle or Steamcore Seal cache.
Follow-up: Unlocks a repeatable Ash Myriad bounty at Greatwood Lodge that increases Ember Sap Bark drop rates.

## [QST-002] Hunt: Prism Collapse
Zone: Glassbloom Run Terraces
Objective:
- Trigger and survive 3 prism traps then defeat 3x BST-002 Veilbound Sentinels.
- Mine 5 Quartzite Bloom clusters after disabling waterfall chills with campfires.
Hooks: Drives players to interact with prism traps and chilled water hazards before mining.
Rewards: 70 silver, +165 regional rep, and a Prismatic Lens Shard satchel with chance at Star-etched Glass.
Follow-up: Opens a weekly delivery to Ashen Ridge Outpost for upgraded sighting scopes.

## [QST-003] Rescue: Tangled Lines
Zone: Morrowpine Tangle Groves
Objective:
- Cut down 3 ensnared scouts before they are dragged off ledges by BST-003 Tangle Wardens.
- Gather 6 Thorn Resin samples from fungal choke points after lighting warding torches.
Hooks: Encourages rope bridge navigation and spore-clearing fire usage.
Rewards: 75 silver, +180 regional rep, and choice of Feathered Skin pack or Living Briar Graft.
Follow-up: Adds a training unlock at Veilreef Camp for ropework recipes.

## [QST-004] Delve: Glowbrood Tide
Zone: Veilreef Cavern Pools
Objective:
- Defeat BST-004 Glowbrood Matriarch while at least one pool remains electrified.
- Collect 4 Iridescent Carapace Fragments and 2 Tidewrought Rings from submerged rune tablets.
Hooks: Highlights pool electrification mechanics and underwater breathing pockets.
Rewards: 80 silver, +195 regional rep, and an Alchemist's Glow Kelp bundle used in conductivity potions.
Follow-up: Unlocks a weekly pearl run to Starfall Inn with boosted matriarch respawn chance.

## [QST-005] Raid: Orchard Meteor Hunt
Zone: Starfall Orchard Shelves
Objective:
- Defeat 1 BST-005 Orchard Worg Alpha within an active starlight shockwave crater.
- Harvest 5 Starfruit clusters and 3 Meteor shard caches before the next shockwave.
Hooks: Forces timing around crater shockwaves and emphasizes scent-mark counterplay.
Rewards: 85 silver, +210 regional rep, and a Meteorbit Fang charm plus Emberfallen Sliver cache.
Follow-up: Opens enchanting sigil crafting at Greatwood Lodge using Emberfallen Slivers.

## [QST-006] Salvage: Lantern Run
Zone: Umberdeep Quarry Galleries
Objective:
- Collapse 3 unstable supports using BST-006 Quarry Brute pillar slams without taking cave-in damage.
- Recover 5 Umber Pebbles and 2 Smuggler's Satchels from fallen carts.
Hooks: Trains players to weaponize Brute behavior and collect Resinous Oil for later explosives.
Rewards: 90 silver, +225 regional rep, and an Umbersteel Pin kit for blacksmithing grinds.
Follow-up: Grants access to hidden cart tunnels leading to the smugglers' stash vendor.
