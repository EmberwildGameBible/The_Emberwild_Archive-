# Quest Expansion Pack

Contracts, hunts, and delivery loops tying new subzones to crafting loops and bestiary research.

## [QST-001] Trail: Ash Vents
Zone: Cinderfen Hot Springs Terraces
Objective: Eliminate 3x BST-001 threats and recover 2 satchels of Ember Sap Bark.
Hooks: Ties into profession nodes and micro-location events; supports Bestiary research unlocks.
Rewards: 50 silver, +100 regional rep, and chance at GEAR-001 bonus cache.
Follow-up: Unlocks repeatable bounty once turned in at Greatwood Lodge.
