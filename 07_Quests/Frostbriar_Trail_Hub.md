# Frostbriar_Trail_Hub

**Origin/Provenance:** Posted by Shiverhook Outwatch at Timberline Post to keep sled routes clear and ferry contracts flowing to [Icefloe Crossing](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#ferry-operator-siva-—-icefloe-crossing).

**Cultural Tie:** Briar-bitten sledders trade favors with Saltmarrow couriers; contracts are stamped with resin thumbprints and echoed by [Slidecaller Orin](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#slidecaller-orin-—-rimebound-relay) horn calls.

**Gameplay Hook:** Hub of 12 grounded quests (4 repeatables, 2 mini chains, 1 capstone) guiding players through Frostbriar hazards, Glimmerdeep hand-offs, and [Ashen Cry Grotto](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md) prep while rewarding specific pulls from [items_table.csv](../08_Items/items_table.csv).

## Quests
1. **Resin Ration Run** — Deliver resin jars and [Briarshot Cartridges](../08_Items/items_table.csv) to Slidecaller Ridge sledders. Repeatable. See also: [Frostbriar_Reach_World_Event](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_World_Event.md), [Icefloe Crossing](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#ferry-operator-siva-—-icefloe-crossing). Spawns [Resin_Ration_Route_Map](../09_Artifacts/Resin_Ration_Route_Map.md).
2. **Snow Squall Sweep** — Cull “White Howl” packs near Shatterpine Outlook using [Coldforged Shortsword](../08_Items/items_table.csv) bait-and-block drills. Repeatable. See also: [Frostbriar_Waykeeper](../03_Lore/Creatures/Frostbriar_Waykeeper.md), [Windbreak_Ice_Lantern](../08_Items/Trinkets/Windbreak_Ice_Lantern.md). Spawns [Snow_Squall_Shard_Log](../09_Artifacts/Snow_Squall_Shard_Log.md).
3. **Timber Slide Repair** — Collect sled bolts and rails after an avalanche; trade [Ironroot Ink](../08_Items/items_table.csv) markers for faster payouts. Repeatable. See also: [Timberline_Sled_Rails](../08_Items/Gear/Timberline_Sled_Rails.md), [Old_Road_Circuit](Old_Road_Circuit.md). Spawns [Slide_Repair_Invoice](../09_Artifacts/Slide_Repair_Invoice.md).
4. **Slidecaller Bells** — Trigger bells to escort a timber sled; timed repeatable rewarding [Lantern Needle](../08_Items/items_table.csv) bundles. See also: [Slidecaller_Bell_Tab](../09_Artifacts/Slidecaller_Bell_Tab.md), [Slidebreaker_Tandem](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Bosses.md). Spawns [Bells_Run_Ticket](../09_Artifacts/Bells_Run_Ticket.md).
5. **Briar Sap Salve** — Craft salves using frost fern resin for chill mitigation; barter [Cinderbrew Flask](../08_Items/items_table.csv) tastes with lodge medics. See also: [Gnat-Smother_Salve](../08_Items/Crafting/Gnat-Smother_Salve.md), [Frostbriar_Stag_Shanks](../08_Items/Cooking/Frostbriar_Stag_Shanks.md). Spawns [Salve_Order_Slip](../09_Artifacts/Salve_Order_Slip.md).
6. **Elk Call Training** — Learn elk calls to soothe Frostbriar herds and mark grazing with [Hearth Map](../08_Items/items_table.csv) sigils. See also: [Frostbriar_Waykeeper](../03_Lore/Creatures/Frostbriar_Waykeeper.md), [Windbreak_Bounty_Board](Windbreak_Bounty_Board.md). Spawns [Elk_Call_Sheet](../09_Artifacts/Elk_Call_Sheet.md).
7. **Meltwater Exchange Run** — Haul [Glimmer Brew](../08_Items/items_table.csv) crates from [Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub](Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md) to Meltwater couriers while escorting [Hearth Map](../08_Items/items_table.csv) scouts. See also: [Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs.md), [Ferry Operator Siva](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#ferry-operator-siva-—-icefloe-crossing). Spawns [Meltwater_Exchange_Scrip](../09_Artifacts/Meltwater_Exchange_Scrip.md).
8. **Mini Chain — Relay Tune-Up (Step 1):** Calibrate Rimebound relay horns with [Lantern Cowl](../08_Items/items_table.csv) padding and sled [Frostfell Vial](../08_Items/items_table.csv) coolant. See also: [Relay_Horn_Permit](../09_Artifacts/Relay_Horn_Permit.md), [Slidecaller Orin](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#slidecaller-orin-—-rimebound-relay). Spawns [Relay_Horn_Tuning_Sheet](../09_Artifacts/Relay_Horn_Tuning_Sheet.md).
9. **Mini Chain — Relay Tune-Up (Step 2):** Deliver tuned horns and [Opal Arrow](../08_Items/items_table.csv) signal packets to [Icefloe Crossing](../07_Quests/NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md#ferry-operator-siva-—-icefloe-crossing) for the next slide hand-off. See also: [Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Settlements.md), [Icefloe_Toll_Ledger](../09_Artifacts/Icefloe_Toll_Ledger.md). Spawns [Relay_Record_Ledger](../09_Artifacts/Relay_Record_Ledger.md).
10. **Mini Chain — Ash Drift Countercharge (Step 1):** Shadow cult envoys in Ash Drift Hollows using [Ashen Pike](../08_Items/items_table.csv) feints to draw scouts out. See also: [Ashen_Cry_Grotto](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md), [Ash_Drift_Hollows](../02_Regions/Frostbriar_Reach/Frostbriar_Reach_Subareas_and_POIs.md). Spawns [Ash_Drift_Patrol_Log](../09_Artifacts/Ash_Drift_Patrol_Log.md).
11. **Mini Chain — Ash Drift Countercharge (Step 2):** Cut supply lines to the grotto entrance and seize [Deepwatch Pauldrons](../08_Items/items_table.csv) crates earmarked for cult shock troops. See also: [Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests](Dungeon/Ashen_Cry_Grotto_Quests.md), [NPC Shops and Inventories](NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md). Spawns [Ashen_Cry_Choke_Map](../09_Artifacts/Ashen_Cry_Choke_Map.md).
12. **Capstone — Timber Syndic Convoy:** Lead a cross-hub convoy carrying [Dawnlit Harpoon](../08_Items/items_table.csv) and [Lantern Harpoon](../08_Items/items_table.csv) sets from Timberline Post to [Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub](Glimmerdeep_Shore_Hub.md); unlocks direct attunement to the [Ashen Cry Grotto entrance](../06_Systems/Dungeons/Ashen_Cry_Grotto/Dungeon_Overview.md). See also: [NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES](NPC_SHOPS_AND_INVENTORIES.md), [Old_Road_Circuit](Old_Road_Circuit.md). Spawns [Syndic_Convoy_Order](../09_Artifacts/Syndic_Convoy_Order.md).
